Recipe boba

Material

1.60-70 grams of tapioca flour

2.60 ml of boiling water

Food coloring

How to make boba

1. Prepare a heat-resistant bowl. Enter tapioca flour and boiling water. Stir well. Cover the dough boba with a damp cloth so as not to dry out.

If using food coloring, add at this step.

2. Take the dough out about 1/8 of a teaspoon. Round shape-round.

3. Prepare a baking sheet, cover with parchment paper. Put the boba in it. Allow to dry for at least 2 hours.

4. Fill the pot with water, bring to a boil. Enter boba already dry. Boba will float when it is half cooked. Boil again for 15-20 minutes. Remove from the stove. Drain.

Boba ready to use to make bubble tea or another dessert.

Flood

Hearing the word flood is already familiar to our ears. Flooding is a natural phenomenon that originates from rainfall with high intensity and duration in watersheds (DAS). Floods occur because of nature and human actions. The causes of natural flooding are erosion and sedimentation, rainfall, river physiographic/geophysical influences, river capacity, land drainage, and tidal effects. The causes of flooding due to human actions are changes in land use, river garbage disposal, densely populated areas along, and damage to flood control buildings.

Mendengar kata banjir memang sudah tidak asing lagi terdengar ditelinga kita. Banjir adalah fenomena alam yang bersumber dari curah hujan dengan intensitas tinggi dan durasi lama pada daerah aliran sungai (DAS). Banjir terjadi karena sebab alam dan tindakan manusia.
