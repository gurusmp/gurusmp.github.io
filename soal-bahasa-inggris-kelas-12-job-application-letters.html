3. 2 types of job application letters


1. Job application letter that is combined with curriculum vitae. In this way, the life history includes the contents of the letter because the contents are combined, this method is also called the combined model.

2. Cover letter separated from curriculum vitae. In this way the curriculum vitae is an attachment and this method is called a separate model.

artinya :

1. Surat lamaran pekerjaan yang digabungkan dengan riwayat hidup (curriculum vitae). Dalam cara ini, riwayat hiidup termasuk isi surat karena isinya berupa gabungan, cara ini juga disebut dengan model gabungan.

Watch the time!

First, pay attention to what time you contact that person. It's not too early or too late, so it's best to call the person between 8:00 and 19:00.

In addition, it is also important to know the schedule of the people you will contact, for example there are people who do not want to be contacted on Sundays because of famtime, or your messages will not be answered at lunchtime.

Call or Text?

Well, actually it's okay to contact someone directly by phone. But, just in case you should make sure via short message first, the goal is to make sure that the person is not busy and can respond to you at the right time.

Text Properly!

Now this is the most crucial and often wrong way to do it by young people today. Called ten at night, use informal language. Fix your message will not be replied to! To avoid that, you need to know the proper way to contact someone you don't know.

1. Say hello or say hello politely.

good manners before expressing the purpose of contacting, you can say hello or say hello, because it will seem to appreciate the person.

For example: Good morning sir, or Assalamualaikum sir

2. Get used to using apologize, excuse me and please.

We don't know the exact schedule of the person we will contact, that's why before starting the introduction and the purpose of your contact, you need to say "sorry to disturb" or "please"

3. Introduce yourself or show your identity clearly.

You don't want your message to be ignored because he doesn't know who is calling. Well, it's not just you who contacted him, so it's really important to say your name and where you come from

For example: I am Rizka from grade 12 IPA 3, or I am Renanta Rizka from Brilio.

4. Note the correct punctuation and capital letters.

You don't have to use the complete PUEBI, match the person you will contact. But imagine you are reading a sentence without commas or periods, people who read it can get confused and even cause misunderstandings. Or you love your credit too much so you cut up the contents of the message. BIG NO!

For example: I want to ask if you will teach an Introduction to Communication class tomorrow? If you don't teach, do you still have any assignments, sir?

5. Explain your purpose for contacting.

explain your needs in a concise, clear, polite and to the point. When making an appointment, it's best not to use commanding sentences, but instead use offer sentences, or ask directly for the time and place.

For example: I am working on an article about an exam, so I need to interview Mr.Rendy to be my resource. Are you willing?

end with thanks

Saying thanks is the same as appreciating the conversation with the person and paying respect, so it's very important to say thank you at the end of a sentence.

Patient is Must!

After obeying the procedures for sending messages to someone you don't know, the next thing is don't terrorize, bomb or send messages over and over again! Apart from being uncomfortable, sending chats repeatedly is also impolite because it seems pushy.

8. Contoh 10 soal pilihan ganda tentang application letters beserta jawabannya


Application letter adalah surat yang dibuat dengan tujuan untuk melamar pekerjaaan.

Generic Sturcture dari Application Letter antara lain:

1. Salutation : Berisi tentang salam pembuka  

2. Introduction : Berisi tentang kalimat pembukaan. Termasuk tujuan menulis surat tersebut serta nama posisi yg dilamar.

3. Body of the letter: berisi tentang data diri pelamar beserta kemampuan-kemampuan yang dimiliki pelamar yang sesuai dengan pekerjaan tersebut.

4. Closing: Berisi harapan-harapan pelamar agar dapat diterima di perusahaan tersebut.

5. Complimentari Close: Berisi salam penutup dan tanda tangan serta nama terang pelamar.

Language Features dari Application Letter:

1. Menggunakan Simple Present Tense

2. Menggunakan simple future untuk memberikan solusi terhadap pekerjaan yang dilamar.

12. Mention Some tips that you should know when writing letters! job application​


Jawaban:

Here are some tips that can help you when writing a job application letter:

Address it to the appropriate person: Make sure you address your letter to the right person or department. Avoid using generic salutations like “To Whom It May Concern.”

Be specific and concise: Your letter should be specific, concise, and to the point. Avoid lengthy paragraphs or irrelevant information.

Highlight your skills and experience: Focus on your skills and experience that are relevant to the job you are applying for. Make sure you highlight your accomplishments and provide examples.

Customize your letter: Customize your letter for each job you apply for. Avoid using a template or generic letter.

Use a professional tone: Use a professional and courteous tone in your letter. Avoid using slang, informal language, or contractions.

Proofread and edit: Before submitting your letter, make sure you proofread it carefully and edit any mistakes. Ask someone else to review it as well to ensure that it is error-free.

Follow up: After submitting your letter, follow up with the employer to ensure they received it and to express your interest in the position.

Overall, it is important to create a positive impression with your letter and showcase your qualifications and interest in the job. A well-written and personalized letter can help you stand out among other candidates and increase your chances of getting an interview.

Penjelasan:

Terjemahan

Berikut adalah beberapa tips yang dapat membantu Anda saat menulis surat lamaran kerja:

Tujukan ke orang yang tepat: Pastikan Anda mengirimkan surat Anda ke orang atau departemen yang tepat. Hindari menggunakan sapaan umum seperti “Kepada Yang Berkepentingan”.

Spesifik dan ringkas: Surat Anda harus spesifik, ringkas, dan to the point. Hindari paragraf panjang atau informasi yang tidak relevan.

Sorot keterampilan dan pengalaman Anda: Fokus pada keterampilan dan pengalaman Anda yang relevan dengan pekerjaan yang Anda lamar. Pastikan Anda menyoroti pencapaian Anda dan memberikan contoh.

Sesuaikan surat Anda: Sesuaikan surat Anda untuk setiap pekerjaan yang Anda lamar. Hindari menggunakan template atau surat generik.

Gunakan nada profesional: Gunakan nada profesional dan sopan dalam surat Anda. Hindari penggunaan bahasa gaul, bahasa informal, atau singkatan.

Koreksi dan edit: Sebelum mengirimkan surat Anda, pastikan Anda mengoreksinya dengan hati-hati dan mengedit kesalahan apa pun. Minta orang lain untuk meninjaunya juga untuk memastikannya bebas dari kesalahan.

Tindak lanjut: Setelah mengirimkan surat Anda, tindak lanjuti dengan pemberi kerja untuk memastikan mereka menerimanya dan untuk menyatakan minat Anda pada posisi tersebut.

Secara keseluruhan, penting untuk menciptakan kesan positif dengan surat Anda dan menunjukkan kualifikasi dan minat Anda pada pekerjaan itu. Surat yang ditulis dengan baik dan dipersonalisasi dapat membantu Anda menonjol di antara kandidat lain dan meningkatkan peluang Anda untuk mendapatkan wawancara.

37. Write An Application Letters Based On Job​


Jawaban:

Dear [Hiring Manager’s Name],

Opening Paragraph (Introduction)

Your cover letter opening should contain a self introduction. Write about who you are, where your expertise lies, where you found the job posting, and why you want to apply for the job.

Body Paragraphs

The second paragraph of your cover letter should respond directly to the job description written by the hiring manager. Describe how your previous job experiences, skills, and abilities will help you meet the company’s needs. To make that easier, you should include exact words and phrases from the job descriptions in your cover letter.

   You can include a bulleted list of your accomplishments

   Make sure to use numbers or percentages to emphasize your bullet points (e.g. Increased online revenue by 13%)

Do some research about the company and find out what their current activities and goals are. Explain how you can help them achieve those goals.

Closing Paragraph

Your cover letter closing is the call-to-action portion of your cover letter. Inform the hiring manager that you’d love to go in for an interview. Provide your email address or other contact information and tell them that you’ll reach out in a week if you don’t hear back. Thank them for spending their time reading your cover letter.

Sincerely,

Your Name
