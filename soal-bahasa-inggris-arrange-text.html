One day, a bear was resting under an orange tree. When he looked up and saw a very big beehive. (Paragraph 3)

"Look how big this beehive is!" thought the bear. "There must be surely be alot of honey in it. Anyway, the bees are small, and would not do me any harm if I take the honey." (Paragraph 1)

As soon as he hit the beehive, a swarm of bees flew out towards him. Some stung his ears, other stung his eyes, some his hands and a few stung his whole body. (Paragraph 2)
