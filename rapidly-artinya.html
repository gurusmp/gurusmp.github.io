7. Mana jawaban yang salah? With the nation emerging from its economic decline. the demand for skilled trainees are increasing rapidly. Sertakan alasan A. With B. Emerging from C. Skilled D. Are


Subject : English
Category : Error Recognition


jawaban :
D. are

kalimat tersebut harusnya ditulis
'With the nation emerging from its economic decline, the demand for skilled trainees IS increasing rapidly.'

yang salah adalah ARE
karena subject kalimat ialah 'the demand', tunggal, maka bentuk kata kerja bantu 'be' yang tepat 'is'.
'are' digunakan bila subjectnya jamak (lebih dari satu).
