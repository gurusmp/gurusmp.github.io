1. Daily activity dg recount text


Last week my friend and I were bored after three weeks of holidays, so we rode our bikes to Pasir Kencana Beach, which is only five kilometres from where I live. When we arrived at the beach, we were surprised to see there was hardly anyone there.

After having a quick dip in the ocean, which was really cold, we realized one reason there were not many people there. It was also quite windy. After we bought some hot chips at the takeaway store nearby, we rode our bikes down the beach for a while, on the hard, damp part of the sand. We had the wind behind us and, before we knew it, we were many miles down the beach.

Before we made the long trip back, we decided to paddle our feet in the water for a while, and then sit down for a rest. While we were sitting on the beach, just chatting, it suddenly dawned on us that all the way back, we would be riding into the strong wind.

When we finally made it back home, we were both totally exhausted! But we learned some good lessons that day.


2. Tolong buatkan text dialog daily activity dengan 3 soal pilihan ganda. Terimakasih


Daily activity adalah kegiatan atau aktivitas yang dilakukan setiap hari. Daily activity bisa juga diartikan sebagai kebiasaaan sehari-hari.

Dalam membuat sebuah daily activity, kita harus menggunakan simple present tense. Itu karena simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah kebiasaan.

Simple present tense mempunyai formula:

S + V1 atau S + V1s/es


V1 untuk I, You, They, dan We

Vis/es untuk He, she dan it.


Pembahasan

Membuat percakapan tentang daily activity beserta 3 soal pilihan ganda:

Teacher: Hello everyone. Today, we are going to discuss about daily activity. First of all, I will ask you what do you do everyday.

Teacher: Rina, what do you do at 6 o'clock every morning?

Rina: I take a bath at 6 o'clock every morning mam.

Teacher: Good. Next, Anton, what do you do every afternoon?

Anton: I always take a nap every afternoon mam. School activity consumes my energy. I need to take a rest after that.

Teacher: Okay Anton, nice answer. Next, Marisa, What do you do before sleeping?

Marisa: I always make my bed, wash my hands and feet and have a pray before sleeping mam. I ask God to protect me during my sleeping.

Teacher: You do it well Marisa. Okay, please open your book on the page 25. We will start the lesson.

26. Buatkan 10 soal daily activity dong ,, segera


To talk about daily activities, we need to use simple present tense. Let’s discuss about simple present tense briefly before we move on to the answer part.

Pembahasan

Simple present is used for actions that happen over and over (repeated actions), true facts, and schedules. Basic pattern of simple present is subject + base verb/ base verb -s/-es.

To make questions: Do/Does + subject + base verb

1. Repeated actions

To talk about repeated actions, we need to use time markers (words about time) that can help to show how often an action happens. Common time markers are: every, once, twice, three times, always, often, usually, sometimes, never. This pattern is good to talk about daily activities.

Note: Always (100%), often & usually (80%), sometimes (50%), rarely (5%-10%), never (0%).

Examples:

- I brush my teeth every day.

- My sister writes diary every night.

- My mother cooks twice a day.

- I usually get up early.

- Doni never arrives late at school.

- Do you do your homework every day?

2. True facts

We can also use simple present to talk about facts or true informations.

Examples:

- Water freezes at 0°C

- Indonesia people celebrates Independence Day on August the 17th.

3. Schedule

Use simple present when we write or talk about our schedule.

Examples:

- My favorite TV show starts at 8:00 pm.

- Our school bus leave the shelter at 6:30 AM and 5:30 PM.

- What time does your school start?

Answers

10 examples of simple present questions for daily activity exercise.

1. What do you do every morning?

Sample answer: I eat breakfast every morning.

2. Do you wash your face every day?

Sample answer: Yes, I do.

3. How often do you play sports?

Sample answer: I play badminton twice a week.

4. Do drink water every day?

Sample answer: Yes, I drink water in the morning and after lunch.

5. What do you eat for breakfast?

Sample answer: I usually eat toast for breakfast.

6. What time is your English class?

Sample answer: My English class is at 1:00 pm on Wednesday and Friday.

7. What do you do after school?

Sample answer: I usually do my homework.

8. Where do you eat lunch at your break time?

Sample answer: I always eat at the school cafeteria.

9. Do you help your mother every day?

Sample answer: Yes, I do. I help my mother to wash dishes after dinner.

10. What do you do before school?

Sample answer: I usually prepare my uniform and check my homework.

Conclusion
