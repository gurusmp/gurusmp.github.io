27. Rearrange the jumbled words to make good sentences. begin with the first word.​


Tony will not clean his room unless his mother tells to.Unless it is about something really urgent Mr. Henz will not see anybody today.You can't go anywhere unless your father gives you permission.The office will open unless it is a holiday.Mrs. Schmidt will not be able to finish her story unless you stop interrupting her.Pembahasan

Ada beberapa hal yang akan kita bahas tentang soal di atas.

Kata kunci dalam soal di atas adalah penggunaan kata "unless".Kata unless bisa kita terjemahkan dengan "kecuali."Contohnya: "The office will open unless it is a holiday" (Kantor akan buka kecuali hari libur.)Padanan kata unless adalah if...not (kalau bukan). Contohnya kita ubah kalimat nomor 4 memakai if...not seperti ini: The office will open if it is not a holiday. (Kantor akan buka kalau bukan hari libur.)Pelajari lebih lanjut
