39. contoh percakapan bahasa inggris


John: How’s it going Anita?
Anita: Yeah, alright. Can’t wait for tonight though!
John: What’s with tonight? Hot date?
Anita: Haha! No, silly. I’m picking up a friend, a girl that is, from the airport. Haven’t seen her since ages! Finally tonight we’re going to catch up.
John: Oh! Where’s she from? She cute? You should introduce us.
Anita: I’m afraid you’re going to be a bad influence for her, so no way!
John: Aaaw, oh well. I hope you’ll have fun tonight.
Anita: Thanks John! Will let you know how it goes.

Dari percakapan di atas, kita bisa mengupas 3 hal.
Sentence Fragments - Can’t wait for tonight though! – She cute?

B : hai..

B : my name is B, and you ?
A : my name is A
B : where is your house ?
A : my house in jln kaliwungu
B : oh i know

40. contoh percakapan bahasa inggris memakai kata pardon


kata pardon digunakan untuk menanyakan suatu perkataan yg tidak terdengar...misal nya, contoh:
anisa:how are you ?
andi: pardon?
kata pardon berarti maaf bisa ulangi lagi?
maksud nya dia kurang dengar apa yang di bilan tadi
