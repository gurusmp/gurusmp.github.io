My Cat
I have a cat. Her name is Kitty. She is a Persian cat. I found Kitty when it was a little alone baby in a town park three months ago. Kitty is now about four months old and it is very attractive.


Kitty has long thick soft black fur. I do care her fur by giving some treatments for her because Kitty has long fur which is different from common cats. Even though Kitty has black fur, but she looks cute and awesome. Kitty has bright blue eyes. The combination between Kitty’s black fur and blue eyes is so perfect when you take pic of it. Kitty has a fit body. I always ask it to play with me. Kitty is so attractive and like to play a yarn ball that I made for it. Besides atractive, Kitty is also a spoiled cat. Whenever Kitty sees me home after school, she just comes at me and jumps on my body. Kitty likes when I touch its body.

I give Kitty a food for cat twice a day and I usually give it a fresh fish at night. Kitty likes to drink a bowl of milk in the morning. Besides giving it healthy foods, I also bring Kitty to a doctor to control its health. Kitty is my lovely pet and it is such an adorable cat ever.

Saya punya kucing. Namanya Kitty. Dia adalah kucing Persia. Saya menemukan Kitty ketika masih bayi sendirian di sebuah taman kota tiga bulan yang lalu. Kitty kini berusia sekitar empat bulan dan itu sangat menarik.

Kitty memiliki tebal bulu hitam lembut panjang. Saya melakukan perawatan bulunya dengan memberikan beberapa perawatan untuk dia karena Kitty memiliki bulu panjang yang berbeda dari kucing biasa. Meskipun Kitty memiliki bulu hitam, tapi dia tampak lucu dan mengagumkan. Kitty memiliki mata biru terang. Kombinasi antara bulu hitam Kitty dan mata biru begitu sempurna ketika Anda mengambil pic dari itu.

Kitty memiliki tubuh yang bugar. Saya selalu meminta untuk bermain dengan saya. Kitty begitu menarik dan suka bermain bola benang yang saya buat untuk itu. Selain atractive, Kitty juga kucing manja. Setiap kali Kitty melihat saya pulang setelah sekolah, dia hanya datang pada saya dan melompat pada tubuh saya. Kitty suka ketika saya menyentuh tubuhnya.

Aku memberikan Kitty makanan untuk kucing dua kali sehari dan saya biasanya memberikan ikan segar di malam hari. Kitty suka minum semangkuk susu di pagi hari. Selain memberikan makanan sehat, saya juga membawa Kitty ke dokter untuk mengontrol kesehatan. Kitty adalah hewan peliharaan saya indah dan itu adalah seperti kucing menggemaskan yang pernah.

6. descriprive text about rabbit, snake, and fish


                                      RABBIT 
Rabbits are cute animals and have a thick hair. They have two eyes, four legs, two ears, and a tail. Their hair has become the main attraction so many people buy rabbits because of their hair color which is really fascinating. You can see a rabbit with a variety of different hair colors such as white, black, brown, gray, and others. One of the most preferred color is white, because of white, rabbit symbolized as a clean animal and absolutely adorable. Rabbit eye is very funny and most of them are back. Some types even have a red color of eye. One of the types of rabbits that have red eyes are Australian rabbits.They have two long ears and even theirs can grow up to 10 cm. With their long ears, they could hear better and know the voices of their predators when approaching. Rabbit ears look so funny and sometimes moving up. Four feet they have are very strong, especially the hind feet because those are used as a point for jump. Front feet have 5 fingers while the hind feet have 4 fingers. Using four legs, they can run very fast and it makes them easier to run away from chasing predators.They have an enough long body. They can grow between 20 to 50 cm with a weight between 0.4 kg to 2 kg. They has a short tail. Their tails looks like a cotton ball because it is so short. A female rabbit is pregnant for 30 days. Every birth, rabbits can deliver between 4 to 12 babies. This is why the rabbit population continues to grow rapidly and they do not become an endangered species. Females can mate since the age of 6 months, while the males when they reach the age of 7 months. After mating, the females will then make their nests in the ground to deliver the babies.Rabbit population continues to grow to this day due to the reproduction done consistently. Rabbits even become an important element in the livestock business. Currently, they become pet and food which are bought and sold continuously throughout the world.
FishFish is a kind of vertebrate animals. Vertebrate animals are kinds of animals that have a backbone. This animal is also included in the group of cold-blooded animals (poikilothermic). Fish has very much species. More than 27,000 species of fish scattered around the world. Based on the type of bones, they are classified into 3 types they are jawless fish (class Agatha, 75 species including lampreys and hagfish), cartilaginous fish (class Chondrichthyes, 800 species including sharks and rays), and the rest belong to bony fishes (class Osteichthyes).They have 3 main parts of body, namely head (Caput), Body (Truncus), and tail (Cauda). Their body is filled with scales that serve to protect its body from the water friction. However, there are some of them that do not have scales for example sharks, dolphins and whales. They also have a lateral line (line lateral) in the form of a line that extends on the surface of its body used for detecting environment condition. Their body shaped like a torpedo makes them move quickly in the water. They use fins to move while its tail serves as a rudder.In general, they breathe through gills. They take oxygen contained in the water. However, there are several types of them which breathe through lungs such as whales, dolphins, and sharks. The fish which is breathing through lungs always comes to the surface to take oxygen from the air. This animal has various shapes and sizes, ranging from the largest such whale shark which reaches14 meters (45 ft.) to the smallest one infant stout fish which is only 7 mm (about 1/4 inch).They can be found in almost all water in the world such as freshwater, brackish and saltwater. These animals can also live at varying depths. Some fishes live near the water surface but others live far away in the bottom surface of the water without light at all. Although they can be found in almost all waters, they cannot live in the lake Great Salt Lake because the lake is very salty even we can float in it.There are 3 ways of reproduction that are oviparous, vivipara, and ovoviviparous. Oviparous fish reproduces by producing egg. This type of fish will attach their eggs on the reef or floating in the water. For examples clown fish that lay their eggs on the anemone. Next is viviparous, this fish bears their children for example dolphins and the last ovoviviparous fish, but the eggs develop in the body and will be out if it has been so large.They are the important food sources for human because they contain high protein for human growth. Currently, they have been breed by humans both marine fish and freshwater fish to be consumed.
