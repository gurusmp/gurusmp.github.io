6. Contoh Asking for Repetition


Answer:

People usually ask for repetition due to the fact that they are not sure, or don't understand the point of the other party's talk. It could also be said because the other party doesn't talk clear or loud enough. Here's the example of asking for repetition.

- can you repeat it please?

- could you say that again?

- say that again?

- come again?

- could you possibly repeat one more time, please?

Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: Questions for repetition

31. Dialog bahasa inggris untuk 4 orang materi asking for repetition, showing attention,showing admiration


Explanation:

Asking for repetition is an expression which is used when we hear something unclearly and want our partner to repeat what they said.
Expression of asking for repetition:
- What did you say?
- Pardon?
- Sorry, can you repeat it?
- I'm sorry, I didn't hear you.
- Sorry, I don't follow you.

Showing attention is an expression which is used to show that you are interested in the topic that your speaking partner is talking about.
Expression of showing attention:
- Really?
- Are you serious?
- Tell me more about it.
- And then what?
- What's next?

Showing admiration is an expression which is used to show that you admire someone's work, achievement, or appearance.
Expression of showing admiration:
- What a...!
- How...!
- It is awesome!
- That is amazing!
- Wow. You look ...!


Answer:
Here's the example dialogue using those expressions above for 4 persons.
Billy: Hey guys, have you heard the news?
Kylie: No.
Tasya: what news?
Mike: *busy with his phone*
Billy: You know Grace Allison? She won the first place at the Chemistry International Competition after beating Bob Flash in the final round.
Tasya: Really?
Kylie: Wow, she is amazing!
Mike: Sorry, what did you say?
Billy: I said Grace Allison won the first place at the Chemistry International Competition.
Mike: She won the first place? Tell me more about it.
Billy: Yeah, she won the first place after beating Bob Flash.
Mike: She beat Bob Flash? That's awesome! I heard he is unbeatable.
Kylie: Nothing is impossible, Mike.
Tasya: Grace must have studied so hard.
Billy: Yeah, I think so.
