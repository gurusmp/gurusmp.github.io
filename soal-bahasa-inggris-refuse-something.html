7. Contoh kalimat bhs Inggris mengandung kata accept dan refuse.


Jawaban:

Verbs:

She accepted my invitation to come to my birthday party.He refused her offer politely.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Verbs (kata kerja) dalam bentuk kalimat Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Terjemahan jawaban:

She accepted my invitation to come to my birthday party. (Dia (cewek) menerima undanganku untuk datang ke pesta ulang tahunku.)He refused her offer politely. (Dia (cowok) menolak tawarannya dengan sopan.)

Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

20. Karangan bahasa Inggris intruction to do something


Instruction to make an orange juice : 1. Prepare a mixer, an orange, sugar and ice cube. 2. Peel the orange and cut it into small pieces, then put it inside the mixer. 3. Pour 3 spoons of sugar and put all the ice cubes to make it fresh. 4. Close the mixer and turn on the mixer. Wait until the orange is blended well. 5. Open the mixer and pour the orange juice in a glass. Your orange juice is ready to serve !
