10. Tuliskan dan Jelaskan tentang simple present continous tense beserta contoh nya !!


Jawaban:

Present continous tense adalah bentuk tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi sedang berlangsung selama waktu tertentu pada waktu sekarang (present). Bentuk ini menunjukkan bahwa aksi tersebut terjadi sebelum (begins before), selama (is in progress at the present), dan berlanjut setelah (continues after) waktu atau aksi lainnya.

Rumus :

Positive (+) Subjek + be (am/is/are) + verb (-ing) + …

Example: 1. She is sleeping right now 2. We are studying today

(-) Negative Subjek +be (am/is/are) + not + verb (-ing) + …

Example: 1. She is not sleeping right now 2.  We are not studying today

(?) Interrogative Be (am/is/are) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?

Example: 1. Is she sleeping right now?  2. Are we studying today?

Simple Present

a) Untuk aksi yang terjadi berulang-ulang atau secara reguler.

Contoh kalimat:

1. Alan reads one book a day.

2. Shella usually walks to her campus.

3. The odd semester starts on September

b) Untuk menyatakan fakta, situasi, dan keadaan yang kita tidak harapkan untuk berubah.

Contoh kalimat:  Shella lives in Bogor.

Present Continuous

a) Untuk aksi yang terjadi selama waktu pembicaraan.

Contoh kalimat:

1. Alan cannot pick up the phone right now because he is reading his book.

b.) Untuk aksi yang berlangsung sekitar waktu pembicaraan

Contoh kalimat:

1. Alan is reading three books a day these days because he needs to catch up to his personal reading goal.

c) Present continuous tense untuk menyatakan situasi dan aksi yang terjadi hanya sementara atau kita harapkan untuk berubah.

Contoh kalimat:  

1. Shella is living in Depok for the time being due to her study.

2. Alan is working at a library this two weeks because his classes are on break.

31. Apa yg dimaksud dengan 1. simple present beserta 5 contoh 2. Past tense beserta 5 contoh 3. Simple present continous beserta 5 contoh​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan ketika suatu kejadian sedang berlangsung saat ini atau kejadian yang berlangsung berulang kali (kebiasaan).

Contohnya:

1.I take the motorcycle to the office

2.The train to Bekasi leaves every hour.

3.George sleeps seven hours every night during the week.

Simple past tense adalah kalimat tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa akhirnya dan telah berakhir di masa akhirnya.

Contohnya:

1. Abdul went to Bali for holiday last Sunday

2. She joined the English club class at school yesterday.

3. Last Monday, Joni got in an accident at the office.

4. I did not sleep well last night.

5. Rani did not come to the office yesterday.

present continuous tense merupakan bentuk tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi sedang berlangsung selama waktu tertentu pada waktu sekarang (present ).

Contohnya:

1. Aunt Christine is warming up the car while Scott looks for his new leather coat.

2. They are eating at Scott’s favorite restaurant today.

3. They are sitting at Scott’s favorite booth, the one with the sparkling red plastic seats.

4. The waiter is standing behind the counter right now with a notepad in his hand and pencil behind his ear.

5. “Are you waiting to open your presents after you eat your pancakes?” said Aunt Christine, taking a sip from her root beer.

39. penjelasan,rumus dan contoh mengenai : simple present,simple past, simple future,present perfect,present continous, past continous


THE PRESENT TENSE The present tense has four forms :

1.   The simple present tense

2.   The present continuous tense

3.   The present perfect tense

4.   The present perfect continuous tense

1.         The simple present tense

The formula :

I/we/you/they He/she/it INFINITIVE INFINITIVE + S It is used do denote : a.  A habitual action (every day action) b.  Unchanging truths about the universe or nature

Example   : - John walks to school (John is in habit of walk-ing to school).

                  - The grass is green (it is unchanging law of nature, that the colour of grass always green).

                  - A aeroplane flies in the sky (it is a simple fact of that an aeroplane can't fly on the ground, they always fly in the sky).

Note        : 1. A verb third singular takes an "s"

                  2. Some verbs in the simple present tense are often qualified by adverbs like :

a.  always, usually, sometimes, often, sel-dom, never and generally. These are placed before the principle verbs, but they are placed after to be.

Example   : - He always gets up early

                - Juliet is always in the room

b.         every day, every morning, every week, every night, etc. These are placed at the beginning of sentence or at the end of it

Example   : - We study every day

                  - Every morning we go to school

2.         The present Continuou Tense

The formula :

TO BE (am, are, is) + PRESENT PARTICIPLE (ING-FORM) It is used to denote an action which is still in progress at the moment of speaking. The rule to form It : ............. to be + verb + ing.

Example  : - The moon is shining now

                  - He is taking a book

Note       : 1.  When we use the present contiuous tense we are not concerned about when an action be-gins when it will end.

                  2.  Certain verbs are practically never used in the present continuous, even when describing the real present.

a.  They are :

                                notice           believe               refuse

                                certain          think                   like

                                own              have                   remember

                                know            belong to           wish

                                include         understand        smell

                                surprise        forget                 need

                                love              appear               fear

                                want             consist of           ...... etc.

                  3.  The present continuous tense can also be used for an action in the immediate future to show intention.

1.         The Simple Past Tense

The formula :

I/we/you/they He/she/it + PAST TENSE 

Example   : - At one o'clock yesterday, you were having lunch.

            :           -           At this time last week, we were answering

It is used to denote an action which began and ended in the past, or to denote that an action happened, or was fi-nished at some time in the past.

Example  : - I studied English diligently yesterday

                  - Andrew found his pen two days ago

Note       : 1.  The simple past tense in regular verb is for-med by adding "ed" to the infinitive :

Infinitive        Simple past

to work           worked

               : 2.  But if verbs ending in "e" add "d" only :

Infinitive        Simple past

to love            loved

               : 3.  There are certain verbs which are not added by "d" or "ed" those are irregular verbs, and you are to memorize them.

                  4.  Yesterday, last and ago are adverbs of time that quality the simple past tense.

I/he/she/it We/you/they WAS + PRESENT PARTICIPLE WERE + PRESENT PARTICIPLE

a.  That an action is still in progress at certain time in the past.

1.         The Simple Future Tense

The formula :

I/we You/they/he/she/it SHALL + INFINITIVE WILL + INFINITIVE

It is used to denote an action happening in future.

Example  : - I shall do my work tomorrow

                  - They will watch football match this after noon

                  - We shall go to the picture next week

                  - He will play this game next day

Note       : a.  To form the simple future tense, usually we use "shall" for the first persons (I and we) and "will" for the other pronouns and pronouns of nouns.

                  b.  Sometime we find that "will" is used for "I and "we" and "shall" for the other pronouns and nouns. In this case it has special function, that is to show the certain wish or intention.
