3. Cerita tentang banjir pakai bahasa inggris


Flood


The word flood means the inability of rivers, lakes, drainage or other waterways to accommodate massive quantities of water, so the water overflow and enters the surrounding area. Flood often occurs in the area on the edge of the river or a region that was once a river absorption, and also in the area where the drainage system is disrupted. This disaster usually occurs in the rainy season as the rainfall with high intensity and with a very long duration. There are two factors that can cause flood, the natural factors and social factors.


The first factor is the natural factor. This factor is a cause that comes from nature itself. Nature produce heavy rain that continued to fall and caused the area of water absorption overflowed, so that the water can no longer flow. As a result, the water will spread to all directions and entered into residential areas. In addition, nature can also cause the constriction of the river as a result of erosion it caused the sedimentation get into the river and reduced the capacity of the river.


The second factor is the social factor. This factor is a factor that often causes flood. It happens because of human behavior itself which often caused nature damage. People often throw the trash in the rivers, it is causing the surface of the river becomes shallow as a result of the trash piling up. Some people also build their houses or other buildings in the area that supposed to be a water absorption area of the river.


The surface of the river that becomes shallow caused the river can no longer accommodate water in the large amounts and also caused the soil can no longer absorb the water. a result, when the rain fall with a long duration, eventhough it is not so heavy it will cause water overflowed towards all directions and the large amount of water will flow into residential areas. The water caused the houses are flooded, even worse flood could sink the houses in the residential.

Dear Rihanna,

Don’t forget, we have had an appointment to go swimming at 3 pm. I’ll be waiting at home. Please be prepared to go after lunch and a nap. Remember to bring your suit. And don’t forget we’ll go there by bicycle. See you later!

Rai

1. When will they go to swim?

a. In the early morning

b. Before midnight

c. After breakfast

d. In the evening

2. Where will Rai waiting for Rihanna?

a. At the park

b. At the swimming pool

c. At her home

d. At the bus stop

3. What should Rihanna do before go to swim?

a. Make an appointment by pool’s website

b. Have a nap and lunch

c. Have a nap while ride a bicycle

d. Have a lunch and bring a swimsuit

Pembahasan

Memo adalah sebuah catatan singkat yang bertujuan untuk mengingatkan seseorang dalam melakukan sesuatu.

Berikut adalah pembahasan untuk masing-masing pertanyaan tentang memo tersebut:

1. Kapan mereka akan pergi berenang?

a. Pagi-pagi buta

b. Sebelum tengah malam

c. Setelah sarapan

d. Di sore hari

Karena pada teks disebutkan bahwa mereka akan pergi pada 3 pm (15.00), maka jawaban yg tepat adalah di sore hari atau in the evening (D).

2. Di mana Rai akan menunggu Rihanna?

a. Di taman

b. Di kolam renang

c. Di rumahnya

d. Di pemberhentian bus

Pada teks, kita bisa melihat ada kalimat “I’ll be waiting at home.” yang berarti aku tunggu di rumahku. Sehingga jawaban yang tepat adalah at her home (C).

3. Apa yang harus Rihanna lakukan sebelum pergi berenang?

a. Membuat janji di website kolam renang

b. Tidur siang dan makan siang

c. Tidur siang sambil naik sepeda

d. Makan siang dan membawa baju renang.

Kita bisa melihat suruhan Rere untuk “Please be prepared to go after lunch and a nap.” Yang berarti tolong bersiap-siap untuk pergi setelah makan siang dan tidur. Sehingga jawaban yang tepat adalah Take a nap and lunch (B).

(A) drops of water which enter through cracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

2. Sinkholes are …

(A) the decorative dripstone features found in caves.

(B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

(C) colorful layers of mineral deposits.

(D) None of the above

Jawaban : (B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

3. Which speleothem grows upward from the floor?

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Sinkholes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (A) Stalagmites

4. An “inclined ceiling” is one which …

(A) is straight.

(B) is crooked.

(C) is slanted.

(D) is wet.

Jawaban : (C) is slanted.

5. Which of the following are NOT caused by dripping water?

(A) Stalactites

(B) Stalagmites

(C) Slopes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (C) Slopes

(D) Geology

Jawaban : (D) Geology

17. cerita malin kundang dalam bahasa inggris berserta amanat dalam bahasa inggris


Once upon a time, on the north coast of Sumatra lived a poor woman and his son. The boy was called Malin Kundang. They didn’t earn much as fishing was their only source of income. Malin Kundang grew up as a skillful young boy. He always helps his mother to earn some money. However, as they were only fisherman’s helper, they still lived in poverty. “Mother, what if I sail overseas?” asked Malin Kundang one day to his mother. Her mother didn’t agree but Malin Kundang had made up his mind. “Mother, if I stay here, I’ll always be a poor man. I want to be a successful person,” urged Malin kundang. His mother wiped her tears, “If you really want to go, I can’t stop you. I could only pray to God for you to gain success in life,” said his mother wisely. “But, promise me, you’ll come home.”

In the next morning, Malin Kundang was ready to go. Three days ago, he met one of the successful ship’s crew. Malin was offered to join him. “Take a good care of yourself, son,” said Malin Kundang’s mother as she gave him some food supplies. “Yes, Mother,” Malin Kundang said. “You too have to take a good care of yourself. I’ll keep in touch with you,” he continued before kissing his mother’s hand. Before Malin stepped onto the ship, Malin’s mother hugged him tight as if she didn’t want to let him go.

It had been three months since Malin Kundang left his mother. As his mother had predicted before, he hadn’t contacted her yet. Every morning, she stood on the pier. She wished to see the ship that brought Malin kundang home. Every day and night, she prayed to the God for her son’s safety. There was so much prayer that had been said due to her deep love for Malin Kundang. Even though it’s been a year she had not heard any news from Malin Kundang, she kept waiting and praying for him.

After several years waiting without any news, Malin Kundang’s mother was suddenly surprised by the arrival of a big ship in the pier where she usually stood to wait for her son. When the ship finally pulled over, Malin Kundang’s mother saw a man who looked wealthy stepping down a ladder along with a beautiful woman. She could not be wrong. Her blurry eyes still easily recognized him. The man was Malin Kundang, her son.

Malin Kundang’s mother quickly went to see her beloved son. “Malin, you’re back, son!” said Malin Kundang’s mother and without hesitation, she came running to hug Malin Kundang, “I miss you so much.” But, Malin Kundang didn’t show any respond. He was ashamed to admit his own mother in front of his beautiful wife. “You’re not my Mother. I don’t know you. My mother would never wear such ragged and ugly clothes,” said Malin Kundang as he release his mother embrace.

Malin Kundang’s mother take a step back, “Malin…You don’t recognize me? I’m your mother!” she said sadly. Malin Kundang’s face was as cold as ice. “Guard, take this old women out of here,” Malin Kundang ordered his bodyguard. “Give her some money so she won’t disturb me again!” Malin Kundang’s mother cried as she was dragged by the bodyguard, ”Malin… my son. 34. Soal bahasa Inggris :Jawab beserta penjelasannya​


Should (Giving Advice)

1. I think they should wait until they finish their study.

2. I think you should stay at home this evening.

3. or should I keep sending his mail to you?

4. You should ask Ivanka, our computer technician, for advice.

5. Everyone should travel.

Should be (Giving Criticism)

1. He should be wearing a coat.

2. It should be open now.

3. Our dog should be running to us [...]

4. They should be coming tonight, [...]

5. They should be in bed. They shouldn't be watching television.

ーーーーーーーーーーーー

Explication

Should adalah sebuah modal verb yang dapat digunakan untuk memberikan saran. Should mengacu pada aksi yang sebaiknya dilakukan di masa depan.

You have wronged her. You should apologize.You look tired. You should rest.

Should juga digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang diharapkan akan terjadi/menjadi kenyataan.

I should graduate next year.The train should arrive in five minutes.

Should be digunakan untuk mengekspresikan sesuatu yang seharusnya terjadi tapi tidak. Should be mengacu pada masa sekarang (present) atau masa depan (future).

You have a fever. You should be in bed, not working.The President should be giving a speech tonight, but he had to cancel it because of the terrorist attack.

Jadi, should be = is/am/are supposed to

in my opinion, this dress suits for your niece so you should buy it.you shouldn't watch this movie because it's boring.

—> contohnya:

I'm going to the market with my mother tomorrow morning so I have to wake up earlier.if she wants to be smarter, she hasn't to play video games too much.

Untuk:

subyek I, you, they, we, names, objects menggunakan have to.subyek he, she, it, name, object menggunakan has to.

PARALLELISM

1. Don't forget to remind me to attend the meeting and to buy the new book in Gramedia. (C)

2. My friends really like playing the piano at home and swimming in Ancol. (D)

3. Mr Tri Joko is well-known not only as a very serious employee but also as a very loyal friend.  (D)

---------------------

EXPLANATION

Parallelism atau parallel construction adalah kesetaraan struktur gramatikal antara dua atau lebih kata, frasa, atau klausa yang digunakan dalam kalimat.

Bandingkan dua kalimat ini:

I learn maths and to speak English at school.

I learn maths and English at school.

Kalimat pertama bukan kalimat yang menggunakan parallelism sedangkan kalimat yang kedua parallel.

Maths dan to speak English mempunyai kedudukan yang setara dalam kalimat tersebut, yaitu sebagai objek dari kata kerja learn. Jadi, seharusnya struktur gramatikal keduanya juga sama.

Dalam kalimat pertama, maths adalah noun, sedangkan to speak English adalah infinitive phrase.  

Kalimat kedua parallel karena maths dan English sama-sama berupa noun.

Parallelism ditandai dengan penggunaan tanda baca koma (,), coordinating conjunction, dan/atau correlative conjunction.

Penggunaan parallelism bertujuan agar suuatu kalimat tidak canggung, mudah dimengerti, dan enak dibaca.

---------------------

EXPLICATION

1. Membeli buku di Gramedia harus parallel dengan to attend the meeting. Jadi, menggunakan infinitive phrase.

2. Berenang di Ancol setara dengan playing the piano. Jadi, gunakan gerund phrase.

3. Teman yang sangat setia setara dengan a very serious employee (article + adverb + adjective + noun). Jadi, frasa yang susunannya sama adalah a very loyal friend.
