3. You work in a tour agency. You see a young gentleman enter your office awkwardly. Offer your service and try to convince him to take one of your holiday packages.

Employee: Is there anything I can help you with, Sir? We are currently still in the vibes of Thanksgiving, so the black friday discount still valid. The last day will be tomorrow.

Customer: Yes, I’m actually planning for a trip to Japan with my wife.

Employee: You may check this package out, sir. With the black friday discount you’ll get 30% off from the normal price. It’s a package for 10 days trip in the 4 stars hotel. The facility is very competitive comparing to other travel agencies since comfort is our priority.

Customer: Hmm.. I’m quite interested with this one.



4. A friend is absent because she is sick. You visit her this afternoon. Your friend needs your help to communicate with the teacher about an assignment that she hasn’t finished yet. Offer her a help.

You: Hey, Lala how do you feel?

Lala: I still need to have some bed rest the doctor said.

You: I see, I hope you’ll get better soon. Oh by the way, what about your English task?

Lala: That’s the problem. I still haven’t finished it and the deadline is tomorrow right? I’m so worried about it.

You: Don’t worry Lala. I will take care of it. Let me talk to Ms. Lidya about your current situation, and I’m sure she will understand.

Lala: Thanks a lot! You are truly the person I can rely on.

You: No worries, a friend in need, is a friend indeed.

Pembahasan

Dialog atau dalam bahasa inggrisnya conversations merupakan percakapan atau komunikasi yang dilakukan oleh dua orang atau lebih untuk membahas hal yang penting maupun tidak. Biasanya dalam dialog bahasa inggris yang terpenting maksud dari dialog itu bisa tercapai tanpa memikirkan grammar yang benar. Ciri - ciri dialog bahasa inggris adalah terjadi di antara dua orang atau lebih, ditulis dalam bahasa lisan atau verbal, dapat dilaksanakan secara langsung ataupun tidak, kalimat sederhana lebih diutamakan. Ada beberapa jenis dialog bahasa inggris yaitu functional conversation, situational conversation dan structural conversation.

29. mohon bantuannya bahasa inggris kelas 12


DIALOG RUMPANG

 

Jawaban  Pendahuluan  

Dalam mengisi soal dialog rumpang, yang perlu diperhatikan adalah:

1. Grammar dalam kalimat tersebut.

2. Part of speech yang hilang atau dibutuhkan.

3. Struktur kalimat tersebut.

 

Pembahasan  

Fill in the blanks ith the correct answer!

You_________(have) to quit your job. How________you_______(feel) about that?

Wife: I__________(be) okay with quitting if it_________(mean) that we________(have) more time together.

Husband: Then I guess we have decided! It is the right decision, isn't it?

Wife: If it_______(not/be), you_______(have/not) that big smile on your face!

Disini kita perlu memperhatikan tenses yang digunakan. Disitu kita tahu bahwa sepasang suami istri sedang membicarakan dan mengambil keputusan agar sang istri mau berhenti dari pekerjaannya. Secara umum percakapan tersebut menggunakan simple present tense. Namun sang istri juga menyatakan sebuah pengandaian dengan conditional tipe 1 karena jika sang istri benar benar keluar demi untuk bersama suami, dia tidak keberatan.

Di akhir percakapan sang istri juga menggunakan pengandaian namun tipe 2 karena kenyataannya sang suami tersenyum lebar karena keputusan sang istri untuk mengiyakan keluar dari pekerjaannya.

Jadi dialognya akan menjadi sebagai berikut:

You have to quit your job. How do you feel about that?

Wife: I will be okay with quitting if it means that we will have more time together.

Husband: Then I guess we have decided! It is the right decision, isn't it?

Wife: If it was not, you would not have that big smile on your face!
