1. Aprilia : Can you help me to do my English home work?

Jodi : ...English is my favorite subject.

Aprilia : Thank you.

A. Let's go

B. I'm sorry, I can't

C. I'd love to, but I dislike English

D. With pleasure

Andi : Sorry,...

A. you're welcome

B. you look very busy

C. I'm busy myself

D. I'm happy you are busy

jawaban: C. I'm busy myself

Marry : Thanks a lot

B. Can you come

C. May you come

D. Don't you come

jawaban: A. Will you come

Aliana : ...I have a lot of of homework.

A. Very good

B. That is a good idea

C. Sorry, I can not

D. I agree with you

jawaban: C. Sorry, I can not

5. Mr. Heri : Attention, please!

Arif : Yes, sir.

The underline word is expression of...

A. asking opinion

B. giving opinion

C. responding attention

D. Showing appreciation

Jawaban: C. responding attention

Students : ...

The best response to complete dialogue above is...

A. Yes, please

B. Yes, Ma'am

C. Yes, Sir

D. Thank you

Jawaban: B. Yes, Ma'am

20. contoh soal kelas 3 bahasa Mandarin bahasa Mandarin satu sampai sepuluh ​


Jawaban:

1 : i : 一

2 : er (huruf e diucapkan seperti pada kata enam) : 二

3 : san : 三

4 : se (huruf e diucapkan seperti pada kata enam) : 四

5 : wuu : 五

6 : lie : 六

7 : sii : 七

8 : ta : 八

9 : ciao : 九

10 : shi : 十

Jawaban:

1 = yi = 一 (huruf y nya dibaca samar)

2 = er = 二 (huruf r nya dibaca samar)

3 = san = 三

4= si = 四

5 = wu = 五

6=liu= 六

7=qi =七 (huruf q dibaca seperti huruf c)

8=ba=八

9=jiu= 九

10= shi=十
