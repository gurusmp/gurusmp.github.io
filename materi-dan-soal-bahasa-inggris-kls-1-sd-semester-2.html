A friend in need is a friend indeed. (JUDUL)

Once upon a time there lived a lion in a forest. One day after a heavy meal. It was sleeping under a tree. After a while, there came a mouse and it started to play on the lion.

Suddenly the lion got up with anger and looked for those who disturbed its nice sleep. Then it saw a small mouse standing trembling with fear. The lion jumped on it and started to kill it. The mouse requested the lion to forgive it. The lion felt pity and left it. The mouse ran away. 

On another day, the lion was caught in a net by a hunter. The mouse came there and cut the net. Thus it escaped. There after, the mouse and the lion became friends. They lived happily in the forest afterwards.

Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense Exercises

questions related to the simple past tense in English. Here are a few sample questions:

Change the following sentences into simple past tense:

a) She plays basketball every weekend.

b) They go to the movies last night.

c) I eat lunch at 12 o'clock.

Fill in the blanks with the appropriate form of the verb in simple past tense:

a) He __________ (watch) a movie yesterday.

b) We __________ (visit) our grandparents last summer.

c) She __________ (dance) at the party last night.

Write the negative form of the following sentences in simple past tense:

a) I bought a new car.

b) They studied for the test.

c) He finished his homework.

Rewrite the following sentences into questions in simple past tense:

a) They went to the beach yesterday.

b) She cooked dinner for her family.

c) He visited his friend last weekend.
