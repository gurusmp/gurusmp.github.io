21. MENYUSUN KATA ACAK MENJADI KALIMAT BAKU​


Menyusun kata

1. binatang kesayangan nya adalah seekor kucing jantan

Kalimat: Her favorite pet is a male cat

male = jantan

pet = hewan peliharaan

2. kaki kelinci kelinci itu pendek tetapi sangat kuat.

Kalimat: The rabbit's legs are short but very strong

Legs = kaki

Short = pendek

strong = kuat

3. rumahnya memiliki sebuah taman yang luas dan indah.

Kalimat: His house has a wide and beautiful park.

wide = luas

has = have = memiliki

beautiful = indah

4. membutuhkan waktu 2 jam untuk mencapai tempat itu.

Kalimat: It takes two hours to reach the place.

Reach = mencapai

take = need = membutuhkan

5. pantai Parangtritis terletak di kabupaten Bantul.

Kalimat: Parangtritis beach is located in Bantul regency.

6. saudara laki-laki temanku belajar di sebuah SMA.

Kalimat: My friend's brother studies at a senior high school.

7. museum itu buka setiap hari, dari jam 9 pagi sampai jam 5 sore.

Kalimat: The museum opens every day,from nine a.m until five p.m.

8. adik perempuanku tidak suka menggambar

Kalimat: my younger sister does not like drawing.

notes:

no 9 dan 10 kerjakan mandiri

Jawaban:

1. male-favorite-her-cat-is-pet-a

Her favorite pet is a male cat

male: jantan

favourite: favorit

her: punyanya ( perempuan )

cat: kucing

2. but-the rabbit's-strong-are-legs-short-very

the rabbit's strong legs are very short

rabbit: kelinci

leg: kaki

strong: kuat

short: pendek

3. has-park-beautiful-a-and-house-wide-his

his house has a beautiful and wide park

his: punyanya ( lelaki )

house: rumah

beautiful: cantik

wide: lebar

park: taman

4. takes-the place-hours-it-two-reach-to

it takes two hours to reach that place

takes: memerlukan

two: dua

hour: jam

place: tempat

reach: menjangkau

5. beach-located-regency-is-Bantul-Parangitis-in

Parangitis beach is located in Bantul regency

beach: pantai

regency: regensi

located: berada di

Parangitis&Bantul adalah nama

6. studies-school-friend's-senior-brother-at-high-school-a-my

My friend's brother studies at a high school

friend: teman

brother: kakak/adik(laki-laki), younger brother: adik kecil laki-laki, older brother: kakak laki-laki

7. every day-until-opens-five p.m.-the museum-nine a.m.-from

Every day the museum opens from nine a.m. until nine a.m.

every: setiap

day: hari

opens: dibuka

museum: museum

until: sampai

8. drawing-my-does-younger-not-sister-like

My younger sister does not like drawing

younger sister: adik perempuan

does not like: tidak suka

drawing: menggambar

9. dance-is-culture-for-its-Bali-famous-and

Bali is famous for its dance and culture

Bali: sebuah pulau

famous: terkenal

culture: budaya

dance: tarian

10. tourists-usually-the temple-by-domestic-bus-or-private-go to-cars

domestic tourists usually go to the temple by bus or private cars

domestic: lokal

tourist: turis

go to: pergi ke

temple: candi

bus: bis

private: privasi/pribadi

car: mobil
