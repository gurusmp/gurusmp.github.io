Librarian: Did you need something?
Rommy: I need your help.
Librarian: How may I help you?
Rommy: I need to find this newspaper article.
Librarian: Have you checked the periodicals section yet?
Rommy: I forgot about that section.
Librarian: If you'd like, I can show you where to find it.
Rommy: Could you do that?
Librarian: Follow me.
Rommy: Thank you for all your help.

Doni: Hi Kiki, how are your today?

Kiki: I am very good, thank you. Doni, look at Santi. She looks ugly this morning.

Doni: Hi Santi, what’s going on with you?

Santi: I’m very sad. My mother doesn’t allow me to write anymore. You know that I love writing very much. My life will be nothing without writing.

Kiki: Why did your mother say to you?

Santi: As we know that our final examination will come soon. My mother asked me to study hard and stop writing for a while.

Yanto: Great! I have the same condition. I will not to play PES anymore.

Doni: Did your mother forbid you too?

Yanto: Yes, I like playing PES every day, I always play games for about 5 hours a day. Now, there is no more time to do that.

Kiki: How a pity you are. Do your hobbies only in your dream man!

Doni: Hey, what do you mean?

Kiki: We are best friend. We love each other. But, we have a big difference. My lovely mother never forbids me to do my hobbies even I’ll have final examination.

Santi: Woww!!! What’s your secret?

Yanto: Yes, tell us. Your method will be tried by us.

Kiki: Listen! I spend my hobby only for two hours a day. I have a deal with my parents. I wouldn’t watch football at night if I have played PES in the afternoon. I commit with my promise.

Doni: Yaph, I got the way. Thank you very much man!

Santi: I get the point. We should reduce the time of doing our hobbies. So, our parents will not see that our hobbies disturb us.

Yanto: Thank you very much Kiki. You’ve given us a solution.
