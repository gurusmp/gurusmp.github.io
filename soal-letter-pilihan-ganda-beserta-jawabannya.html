Pembahasan

The Post Meridian Restaurant

NOW HIRING

Are you friendly? Do you work well with other people? Then join the front-office team at the Post Meridian Restaurant. We are currently hiring wait staff.

Servers are responsible for the following duties:

• Working with the host or hostess to help customers to their seats

• Putting utensils and napkins on tables before customers arrive

• Explaining the different dishes on the menu to customers

• Taking food orders

• Bringing orders to kitchen staff

• Delivering food from the kitchen to customers

• Helping bussers clear tables during busy shifts

• Following directions from the head waiter

For more information, call Jim Paisley at 555-1907.

1. False. Servers tidak memberi arahan (giving direction) kepada bussers, mereka hanya membantu bussers membersihkan meja (clear tables).

2. True. Servers bekerja bersama host atau hostess untuk membantu pelanggan ke tempat duduk mereka.

3. True. Saat sibuk (on a busy night), banyak karyawan yang membantu membersihkan meja.

To whom it may concern,


I’m applying for the Project Manager position advertised through Jakarta Post. As shown in the enclosed resume, I have a strong academic background in Chemical Engineer graduated from Universitas Indonesia on 2007.


My current work as Project Management in various multinational company has given me firsthand experience in the challenge associated with the development of a successful Petrochemical Company. In addition, I have excellent managerial and communication skills both in Bahasa Indonesia as well as English.


This background, combined with over ten years of Ethylene Plant Operation as Project Management, has given me a unique perspective that would be particularly valuable in meeting the responsibilities of this position, as described in your position. 6. Sebutkan contoh soal business letter dan jawabannya


Jawaban:

Business letters adalah jenis surat yang biasanya digunakan dalam perihal perniagaan atau administrasi perkantoran. Ada beberapa jenis business letters yang termasuk sering digunakan seperti letter of inquiry, letter of complaint, letter of apology, letter of order and request dan lainnya.

Berikut ini adalah salah satu contoh business letter. (Letter of inquiry)

Dear Mr.Villalibre,

I am writing to enquire in relation to one of your instagram account which offers the football boots product, 'Casa de Villalibre'. I am interested in conducting discussion on the collaboration with my company which runs the football academy for the young adult. I wonder if you could inform me the details of your product mentioned in your social media and schedule an online meeting this week.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Best Regards,

Ahmed Al-Hilal

Penjelasan:

Berikut contoh soalnya:

1. What does the sender want to ask to Mr.Villalibre? The permission to discuss about the collaboration project and the details of the product.

2. Which platform does Mr.Al-Hilal know about Mr.Villalibre's product?

He knows it from Instagram.

3. When is the suggested online meeting expected to be held? This week.

4. What is the meaning of 'conduct'?

It means to 'hold' or 'do'.

Q : what is the structure of personal letter?
A  : 1. Date : Date when the letter is writen (top left).
      2. Address : Place where you are writing fron (top right).
      3. Salutation & Name : Greeting and the person’s name you are writing to.
      4. Introduction : The opening of the letter usually starts with “how are you?”  or refers to previous letter.
      5. Body : The main part of the letter. It includes what you want to write to the other person.
      6. Closure : The part indicates the letter is going to end.
      7. Complimentary close : Short expressions like “love you” , “sincerely yours” , “love”.
      8. Signature : Signature or initials of the writer.
      9. Postscript P.S : After thougt in a letter. You begin with P.S. and end it with your initials.

33. PILIHAN GANDA Keluaran dari program berikut adalah ? for letter in 'hello': if letter == 'e' or letter == 's': break print(letter) a.se b.es c.s d.e e.hello Ada yang tau jawabannya apa?


Jawaban:

Jawabannya D.E

Penjelasan:

Program di atas akan mencetak huruf-huruf dalam kata "hello" satu per satu. Namun, ketika program mencapai huruf 'e', kondisi `if letter == 'e' or letter == 's':` akan dievaluasi. Karena 'e' terpenuhi sebagai huruf yang sama dengan 'e', program akan keluar dari loop `for` menggunakan pernyataan `break`.

Akibatnya, program hanya akan mencetak huruf 'h' dan 'e' sebelum keluar dari loop. Sebagai hasilnya, output yang dihasilkan adalah "he" dan bukan "hello" secara keseluruhan. Oleh karena itu, jawaban yang benar adalah d. e.
