V.balok + V. balok

Volume balok ¹

= P × l × t

= 60cm × 15cm × 15cm

= 13.500 cm³

Volume balok ²

= P × l × t

= 20cm × 15cm × 20cm

= 6.000 cm³

Volume bangun gabungan

= Volume balok + Volume kubus

= 13.500 + 6.000

= 19.500 cm³
