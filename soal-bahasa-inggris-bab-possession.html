a. I have a pen pal. My pen pal is very kind.

Pembahasan

Possessive adjectives adalah kata ganti yang menyatakan kepemilikan dari benda

Fungsi dari possessive adjective adalah menerangkan kepemilikan sesuatu, letaknya selalu sebelum kata benda (noun)

Penulisannya adalah possessive adjective+noun

Contoh:

My mother buys me a bag. (Ibu saya membelikan saya sebuah tas)

My bag is beautiful. (Tas saya cantik)

Her brother is coming (Kakaknya sedang datang)

39. She regressed to her old bad habits. Best word to replace "regressed" without changing the meaning of the sentence is A. returned an earlier state. B. progressed to a better situation C. transgressed set rules D. reclaimed lost possession


VOCABULARY AND READING COMPREHENSION

Regressed =  returned an earlier state

----------------

EXPLANATION

Regress is the preterite of the verb regress.

Regress is from the Latin present infinitive regradi.

The Latin prefix re- means back or backwards.

The Latin present infinitive gradi comes from the verb gradior that means (I) go.

So, regress means go back = return

Therefore, the best word to replace "regressed" without changing the meaning of the sentence is (A) returned an earlier state.
