5. carilah kalimat finite verb atau non-finite verb dari teks yang saya lampirkan di atas, dan berikan alasannya mengapa kalimat itu sebagai finite verb atau non-finite verb, dan jelaskan bagian mana finite verb and non-finite verb


•ᴥ• Materi: Finite, Non-Finite Verb

–·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·––·–

•ᴥ• Finite Verb

Batara Kala WAS very angry with Batara Surya and Batari Chandra. He TRIED to eat them. But the God and Goddess ESCAPED. Batara Kala GOT angrier. Since then he HAS never stopped chasing them. Batara Wisnu WANTED to protect the God and the Goddess and (he) ORDERED the people on Earth to make a lot of noise when eclipse OCCURS. The noise WOULD help Batara Surya and Batari Chandra escape from Batara Kala's throat.

•ᴥ• Non-Finite Verb

Batara Kala was very angry with Batara Surya and Batari Chandra. He tried TO EAT them. But the God and Goddess escaped. Batara Kala got angrier. Since then he has never STOPPED CHASING them. Batara Wisnu wanted TO PROTECT the God and (he) the Goddess and ordered the people on Earth TO MAKE a lot of noise when eclipse occurs. The noise would HELP Batara Surya and Batari Chandra escape from Batara Kala's throat.

PEMBAHASAN MATERI

Apa itu Finite Verb?

Finite Verb merupakan kata kerja utama pada suatu kalimat baik pada independent maupun dependent clause yang hanya terdapat pada bentuk waktu simple present tense dan simple past tense. Finite Verb juga mencakup auxiliary verbs seperti to be dan modals, seperti:

To be ⇆ are, am, is, was, wereModals ⇆ can, could, may, should, might

Lalu, apa itu Non-Finite Verb?

Non-Finite Verb merupakan kata kerja yang tidak berperan sebagai verb, melainkan sebagai noun (kata benda), adjective (kata sifat), ataupun adverb (keterangan). Non-finite verb terbagi menjadi tiga, yaitu:

Gerund (Verb-ing) To Infinitive dan Bare InfinitiveParticiple (Present P: We are making cookies, Past P: We have made cookies)

PENJELASAN SOAL

•ᴥ• Finite Verb

Batara Kala WAS very angry with Batara Surya and Batari Chandra. ⇆ WAS disebut sebagai Finite Verb karena berfungsi sebagai kata kerja utama (To be). He TRIED to eat them. ⇆ TRIED berfungsi sebagai kata kerja utama pada bentuk simple past tense, sehingga ia masuk ke dalam jenis Finite Verb.But the God and Goddess ESCAPED. ⇆ ESCAPE berfungsi sebagai kata kerja utama pada bentuk simple past tense, sehingga ia masuk ke dalam jenis Finite Verb.Batara Kala GOT angrier. ⇆ GOT berfungsi sebagai kata kerja utama pada bentuk simple past tense, sehingga ia masuk ke dalam jenis Finite Verb.Since then he HAS never stopped chasing them. ⇆ HAS disebut sebagai Finite Verb karena berfungsi sebagai kata kerja utama (Auxiliary modal verb). Batara Wisnu WANTED to protect the God and the Goddess and (he) ORDERED the people on Earth to make a lot of noise when eclipse OCCURS. ⇆ WANTED dan ORDERED disebut sebagai Finite Verb karena fungsinya sebagai kata kerja utama pada bentuk simple past tense, sedangkan OCCURS merupakan bentuk waktu simple present tense yang juga sebagai kata kerja utama dan termasuk ke dalam Finite Verb.The noise WOULD help Batara Surya and Batari Chandra escape from Batara Kala's throat. ⇆ WOULD disebut sebagai Finite Verb karena berfungsi sebagai kata kerja utama (Auxiliary modal verb).

•ᴥ• Non-Finite Verb

Batara Kala was very angry with Batara Surya and Batari Chandra. ⇆ Tidak terdapat Non-finite Verb pada kalimat ini. He tried TO EAT them. ⇆ TO EAT merupakan To Infinitive yang masuk ke dalam jenis Non-finite Verb. But the God and Goddess escaped. ⇆ Tidak terdapat Non-finite Verb pada kalimat ini. Batara Kala got angrier. ⇆ Tidak terdapat NON-FINITE VERB pada kalimat ini. Since then he has never STOPPED CHASING them. ⇆ STOPPED dan CHASING sama-sama masuk ke dalam jenis Non-finite Verb yang terletak setelah auxiliary modal verb 'has'. STOPPED merupakan Past Participle sedangkan CHASING merupakan Gerund. Batara Wisnu wanted TO PROTECT the God and the Goddess and (he) ordered the people on Earth TO MAKE a lot of noise when eclipse occurs. ⇆ TO PROTECT dan TO MAKE merupakan To Infinitive yang masuk ke dalam jenis Non-finite Verb.  The noise would HELP Batara Surya and Batari Chandra escape from Batara Kala's throat. ⇆ HELP merupakan Bare Infinitive yang terletak setelah auxiliary modal verb 'would' dan masuk ke dalam jenis Non-finite Verb.

Here, I will give brief explanation about “Too and Enough”.

-          TOO is used before adjective.

It is used to say that something is more than we need or want.

In Indonesian TOO means terlalu.

Formula        : too + adjective

Example       :

- The road is too slippery. you will fall.

- These brownies is too sweet. I don’t really like it.

 

-          ENOUGH is used after adjective or before noun.

it is used to say that something is as much as we need or want.

In Indonesia, ENOUGH means cukup.

Formula        : adjective + enough / enough + noun

Example       :

-                      Is your mark good enough this semester?

-                      Do you have enough money to go to Spain?

14. Contoh soal non finite clause​


Jawaban:

In linguistics, a non-finite clause is a dependent or embedded clause that represents a state or event in the same way no matter whether it takes place before, during, or after text production.[1] In this sense, a non-finite dependent clause represents one process as a circumstance for another without specifying the time when it takes place as in the following examples:

Non-Finite Dependent Clauses

I'm going to Broadway to watch a play.

I went to Broadway to watch a play.

Finite Dependent Clauses

I'm going to Broadway so I can watch a play.

I went to Broadway so I could watch a play.

Similarly, a non-finite embedded clause represents a qualification for something that is being represented as in the following examples:[2]

Non-Finite Embedded Clauses

I'm on a street called Bellevue Avenue.

I was on a street called Bellevue Avenue.

Finite Embedded Clauses

I'm on a street that is called Bellevue Avenue.

I'm on a street that used to be called Bellevue Avenue.

I was on a street that is called Bellevue Avenue.

I was on a street that used to be called Bellevue Avenue.

23. Finite clause and non finite clause


Finite verbs

Finite verbs are a form of verb that contains tenses: present or past, influenced by agreement with the subject, also influenced by number or number (singular/plural).

contoh:

She goes to school.

→ kata goes merupakan finite verb karena digunakan dalam present tense dan terikat dengan subject she

Non-finite verbs

Non-finite verbs merupakan kebalikan dari Finite verbs, yaitu suatu bentuk kata kerja yang tidak mengandung tenses: present atau past.

Non-finite verbs cannot stand alone like finite verbs, therefore non-finite verbs can be changed into verbs in the form of participles, gerunds, infinitives (to and bare).

contoh:

She asked me to send this letter. → the word to send is a non-finite verb because it is not bound by tenses, it also has an additional particle to which is a form of to infinitive

37. finite and non finite clause

38. Change the finite clause below into non-finite clause 1. Finite clause: Vira drives her car and She goes to Malang Alone Non-Finite Clause: ...............


Non-finite Clause

Driving her car, Vira goes to Malang alone.

---------------------

EXPLANATION

Non-finite clause adalah klausa yang kata kerjanya tidak menunjukkan tense. Sebuah non-finite clause subjeknya tidak disebut karena sama dengan induk kalimatnya.

Sebuah non-finite clause kata kerjanya menggunakan bentuk

1. (to) infinitive jika menunjukkan maksud/keinginan untuk melakukan suatu aksi (aksi belum terjadi) .

contoh: He wants to get married next year.

2. gerund/present participle menyatakan sesuatu yang terjadi bersamaan dengan main clause, terjadi baru saja sebelum main clause, penyebab terjadinya main clause, atau hasil dari main clause.

Contoh:

Whistling happily, he left the office.

Realizing the money's gone, I call the police.

Not understanding the question, he failed the test.

The building collapsed, killing three people.

3. perfect participle untuk menekankan bahwa anak kalimatnya terjadi sebelum main clause.

Contoh: Having eaten a whole pan of pizza, he felt bloated.

4. Past participle jika non-finite clause nya bermakna pasif.

Shocked by the news, my mother fainted. (My mother was shocked by the news and fainted)

----------------

EXPLICATION

Vira drives her car and she goes to Malang alone.

Kedua klausa ini terjadi bersamaan karena kedua finite verbnya dalam present simple. Jadi, gunakan present participle (v-ing) non-finite clause.
