4. soal bahasa inggris kelas 10​


Jawaban:

1. The writer had an exhilarating and unforgettable experience watching a live football match at the stadium, particularly the World Cup 2010 Quarter Final between Ghana and Uruguay in South Africa.

2. The described match is the World Cup 2010 Quarter Final between Ghana and Uruguay, ending 1-1 after extra time, with Uruguay progressing to the Semifinals after winning 4-2 via a penalty shootout.

3. A suitable title based on the text above could be "Unforgettable World Cup 2010 Quarter Final: Ghana vs. Uruguay."

4. Paragraph 2 expresses the writer's disbelief and excitement about being at the stadium in South Africa, watching the World Cup live after previously watching it on TV at home. The writer describes the surreal feeling of being present at such a significant event.

5. The writer wrote the text to share their personal experience and emotions during the World Cup 2010 Quarter Final match between Ghana and Uruguay, highlighting the passion, excitement, and disappointment felt during the game. It serves as a recounting of a memorable football event and the writer's strong support for Ghana.

Answer:

1.The writer had an amazing and unforgettable experience.

2.The match described in the text is a football match at the World Cup           2010 Quarter Final between Ghana and Uruguay.

3.My Amazing Experience at World Cup 2010

4.Paragraph 2 tells us that the writer is amazed that they are actually at the stadium rather than at home watching from their TV screen.

5.To tell his experience at the World Cup 2010.
