PEMBAHASAN

Simple past adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau.

Rumus :

(+) S + Verb-2 + O

(-) S + Did + Not + Verb-1 + O

(?) Did + S + Verb-1 + O

Tanda waktu :

YesterdayTwo (weeks) agoLast (year)Three (hours) agoEtc

Contoh :

I bought some books yesterday.I did not buy some books yesterday.Did I buy some books yesterday?

Past continuous adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sedang berlangsung pada masa lampau di saat peristiwa lain terjadi/dilakukan.

Rumus :

(+) S + To be (was/were) + Verb-ing + O

(-) S + To be (was/were) + not + Verb-ing + O

(?) To be (was/were) + S + Verb-ing + O

Tanda waktu :

....when.......as........while...All day yesterdayEtc

Contoh :

She was listening to the radio when I called her.While I was cooking, my sister did her homework.

Past perfect adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sudah selesai dilakukan pada waktu lampau.

Rumus :

(+) S + Had + Verb-3 + O

(-) S + Had + not + Verb-3 + O

(?) Had + S + Verb-3 + O

Tanda waktu :

....before........after......as soon as.......until...

Contoh :

Before I arrived, The train had left.She told me her name after I had asked her twice.

20. kak tolong dong buat 10 soal past perfect tense dan jawabanya+pembahasannya setiap jawabanmakasih


Past perfect tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi telah selesai pada suatu titik di masa lalu sebelum aksi lainnya terjadi. Past perfect tense memiliki rumus sebagai berikut =

(+) Subject + had + verb 3/past participle(-) Subject + had + not + verb 3/past participle(?) Had + Subject + verb 3/past participle

Contoh kalimat =

1.

(+) You had studied physics before you move to China. ( kamu telah belajar fisika sebelum kamu pindah ke Cina)(-)  You had not studied physics before you move to China. (kamu belum belajar fisika sebelum kamu pindah ke Cina)(?) Had you studied physics before you move to China. (sudahkah kamu belajar fisika sebelum kamu pindah ke Cina?)

2.


(+) My mother had slept before I came. (ibuku telah tidur sebelum saya datang)(-) My mother had not slept before I came. (ibuku belum tidur sebelum saya datang).(?) Had my mother slept before I came? (sudahkah  ibuku tidur sebelum saya datang?).

3.

(+) Rina had finished it when he came her house. (Rina telah menyelesaikannya ketika dia datang ke rumahnya).(-) Rina had not finished it when he came her house. (Rina belum menyelesaikannya ketika dia datang ke rumahnya).(?) Had Rina finished it when he came her house? (sudahkan Rina menyelesaikannya ketika dia datang ke rumahnya?)   

4.

(+) She had eaten when I called her. ( Dia telah makan ketika saya memanggilnya).(-) She had not eaten when I called her. (Dia belum makan ketika saya memanggilnya).(?) Had she eaten when I called her? (sudahkah dia makan ketika saya memanggilnya?)

5.

(+) Before I asked her, she had taken bath. (sebelum saya memintanya, dia telah mandi).(-) Before I asked her, she had taken bath. (sebelum saya memintanya, dia belum mandi).(?) Had she taken bath before I asked her? (sudahkah dia mandi sebelum saya memintanya?).

6.

(+) Alan said that he had gone to school when Sinta called him. (Alan berkata bahwa dia telah pergi sekolah ketika Sinta menelponnya).(-)  Alan said that he had not gone to school when Sinta called him (Alan berkata bahwa dia belum pergi sekolah ketika Sinta menelponnya)(?) Had Alan said that he gone to school when Sinta called him? (sudahkan Alan berkata bahwa dia pergi sekolah ketika Sinta menelponnya?)

(+) He had gone home before Miss Anna finished her lesson. (dia telah pulang sebelum bu anna menyelesaikan pembelajarannya).(-) He had not  gone home before Miss Anna finished her lesson. 23. kalimat past tense beserta contohnya


my cat was killed Last night

Kalimat past tense beserta contohnya. Past tense pada dasarnya adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan kejadian yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau atau kejadian yang sudah terjadi.

Ada beberapa tipe past tense yakni

(1)    Simple Past Tense

(2)    Past Continuos/ Progressive Tense

(3)    Past Perfect Tense

(4)    Past Perfect Continuous Tense.

 

Contoh kalimat Simple Past Tense

I ate an apple yesterday.

Formula : Subject + Verb 2

Subject = I

Verb 2 = ate

 

Contoh kalimat Past Continuous Tense

I was eating an apple yesterday.

Formula : Subject + was/were + verb-ing

Subject = I

was/were : “was” digunakan karena subjectnya “I”

Verb-ing = eating

 

Contoh kalimat Past Perfect Tense

I had eaten an apple before I eat an orange.

Formula : Subject + had + verb 3

Subject = I

had

Verb 3 = eaten

 

Contoh kalimat Past Perfect Continuous Tense

I had been eating an apple since 10.00am.

Formula : Subject + had + been + verb 3

Subject = I

had

been

Verb –ing = eating

27. pengertian simple past tense beserta contoh


Simple Past adalah Tenses yg menunjukkan kejadian/peristiwa di waktu/masa lampau dan selesai di masa/waktu lampau juga.

Simple Past yg berbentuk Verbal (yg ada kata kerja/verb)

(+) Subject + V2 + Object

= I play a ball yesterday

(Saya bermain sebuah bola kemarin)

(-) S + did not + V1 + Object

= I did not play a ball yesterday

(Saya tidak bermain bola kemarin)

(?) did I play a ball yesterday

(Apa saya bermain sebuah bola kemarin?)

Simple Past bentuk Nominal (yg tak ada kerja/ tanpa verb)

(+) Subject + was/were + adjective/adverb/noun

= I was so happy yesterday  

(saya sangat bahagia kemarin)

(-) S + was/were + not + object (adj/adv/noun)

= I was not happy yesterday

(Saya tak bahagia kemarin)

(?) was/were + S + O (adj/adv/noun)

= was I happy yesterday?

Time signal (waktu yg menunjukkan Past Tense)

= yesterday, last, ago dll

Present Tense

1. Verbal Sentence

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O

= You read a book everyday

(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O

= You do not read a book everyday

(?) do/does + S + V1 + O?

= do You read a book everyday?

2. Nominal Sentence

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + O

= You are a student now

(-) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + O

= You are not a student now

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + S + O?

= are you a student now?

Past Tense

1. Verbal Sentence

(+) S + V2 + O

= I opened the door yesterday

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

= I did not open the door yesterday

(?) did + S + V1 + O?

= Did I open the door yesterday

2. Nominal Sentence

(+) S + Tobe (Was/were) + O

= You were a student last year

(-) S + Tobe (Was/were) + not + O

= You were not a student last year

(?) Tobe (Was/were) + S + O?

= were you a student last year?

Penjelasan:

Pengertian Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense adalah suatu bentuk kata kerja sederhana untuk menunjukkan bahwa suatu kejadian terjadi di masa lampau.

Pada simple past tense, waktu kejadian (yesterday, last two days, last year) atau periode waktunya (for two months, for a day, for an hour) dapat disebutkan secara spesifik. Simple past tense juga dapat digunakan untuk membentuk conditional sentence tipe 2

Simple Future Tense

Rumus Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense dibentuk dari verb-2 (past tense) berupa kata kerja biasa atau verb “to be”. Verb-2 merupakan bare infinitive (bentuk dasar verb) dengan tambahan -ed, -en, -d, -t, -n, atau -ne untuk regular verb atau bentuk yang tidak konsisten pada irregular verb, sedangkan pada verb “to be”, verb-2 berupa was dan were.

Regular dan Irregular Verb

Was yang merupakan singular verb digunakan pada singular subject berupa singular noun (seperti: Andi, book, dan cat) dan singular pronoun (seperti: I, she, he, dan it) kecuali “you”, sebaliknya were yang merupakan plural verb digunakan pada plural subject berupa plural noun (seperti: cats, people, books), plural pronoun (seperti: you, they, we, dan cats), compound subject dengan kata hubung “and” (seperti: you and I, Andi and Susi), dan you.

Subject-Verb Agreement

rumus simple past tense: S (singular/plural) + verb-2 *mengunakan verb-2 berupa verb "to be": S (singular subject kecuali "you") + was / S (you / plural subject) + were

Past Tense ada beberapa macam :

1. Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense adalah tenses yang berfungsi untuk menunjukkan bahwa suatu kejadian terjadi di masa lampau.

Rumus dan Contoh Kalimat Simple Past Tense

S + verb-2/be (was/were)

The party started at 10.00 a.m.

(Pesta dimulai jam 10 pagi.)

I was really busy yesterday.

(Saya benar-benar sibuk kemarin.)

2. Past Continuous Tense

Past continuous tense digunakan untuk mengungkapkan bahwa suatu aksi sedang terjadi pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Rumus dan Contoh Kalimat Past Continuous Tense

S + was/were + present participle (-ing)

The team was playingbasketball all day yesterday.

(Tim bermain basket sepanjang hari kemarin.)

3. Past Perfect Tense

Past perfect tense untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi telah selesai pada suatu titik di masa lalu sebelum aksi lainnya terjadi.

Rumus dan Contoh Kalimat Past Perfect Tense

S + had + past participle (verb-3)

When he came last night, the cake had run out.

(Ketika dia datang semalam, kue sudah habis.)
