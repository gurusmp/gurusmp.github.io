2. berikan jawaban latihan soal pat /ukk kelas 8 Bahasa Inggris beri kunci jawaban nya​


Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

Semoga soal-soal ini bermanfaat untuk latihan bahasa Inggris Anda!

18. bahasa Inggris nya mawar, melati ​


Answer:

Bahasa Inggrisnya mawar yaitu rose.Bahasa Inggrisnya melati yaitu jasmine.

Explanation

Bunga dalam bahasa Inggris adalah flower (singular) atau flowers (plural) dan termasuk elemen obyek kalimat/kata benda (noun). Contohnya sebagai berikut:

Mawar: RoseMelati: JasmineBunga lili: Lily Bunga kamboja: CambodiaBunga dahlia: DahliaBunga matahari: SunflowerTeratai: LotusBunga sakura: Cherry blossomAnggrek: OrchidKaktus: Cactus

Example of sentences:

In our garden, there are roses and jasmine (Di kebun kami, terdapat bunga mawar dan melati).Roses are divided into several types, for example: white roses, red roses, pink roses, etc (Bunga mawar terbagi menjadi beberapa jenis, misalnya: mawar putih, mawar merah, mawar pink, dan lainnya).Jasmine flowers are white, the aroma is very fragrant and pungent (Bunga melati berwarna putih, aromanya harum dan menyengat).

29. tolong deskripsi kan bunga melati dalam bahasa inggris


Jasmines can be either deciduous (leaves falling in autumn) or evergreen (green all year round), and can be erect, spreading, or climbingshrubs and vines. Their leaves are borne opposite or alternate. They can be simple, trifoliate, or pinnate. The flowers are typically around 2.5 cm (0.98 in) in diameter. They are white or yellow in color, although in rare instances they can be slightly reddish. The flowers are borne in cymose clusters with a minimum of three flowers, though they can also be solitary on the ends of branchlets. Each flower has about four to nine petals, two locules, and one to four ovules. They have two stamens with very short filaments. The bracts are linear or ovate. The calyx is bell-shaped. They are usually very fragrant. The fruits of jasmines are berries that turn black when ripe.[6][7]The basic chromosome number of the genus is 13, and most species are diploid (2n=26). However, natural polyploidy exists, particularly in Jasminum sambac (2n=39), Jasminum flexile (2n=52), Jasminum primulinum (2n=39), and Jasminum angustifolium (2n=52).[6]
