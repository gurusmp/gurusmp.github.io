4. Penggunaan kata some,many,much pada bahasa inggris menyatakan jumlah .... beserta pengertiannya​


penggunaan kata some :

"some" memiliki arti "beberapa", atau "sedikit". ini digunakan untuk countable dan uncountable nouns, dan hanya untuk kalimat positif.

contoh :

- i have some milk

- i eat some apples

kebalikannya yaitu "any". sama seperti tadi, tapi ini digunakan hanya untuk kalimat negatif. dan juga digunakan untuk kalimat tanya.

contoh :

- i don't have any money

- he doesn't eat any meat

penggunaan kata many :

"many" memiliki arti "banyak". ini digunakan hanya untuk countable nouns.

contoh :

- he read many books at the library

- how many cows are there in the farm?

penggunaan kata much :

"much" memiliki arti "banyak". ini digunakan hanya untuk uncountable nouns.

contoh :

- he drinks much water

- how much time do we have left?

10. Cerita bahasa inggris A SMALL THINGS COST MUCH beserta pertanyaan


A Small Things Cost Much


One day, while walking down the road, a father said to his son, "Look, a horseshoe is lying in the mud. Pick it up."

"I hate to pick up things from mud."

The father picked up the horseshoe and kept it in his pocket. On reaching the village, he sold the horseshoe and bought some cherries.
He understood the desire of his son and dropped a cherry on the ground. His son picked it up, washed and ate it. 

Then the father said to him, "Sometimes even small things cost much. These cherries are because of that horseshoe that you refused to pick up from the mud."

And from that his son understood what his father meant.

Questions:
» Why did the son not want to take the. horseshoe?
» What did the father do when he and his son reached the village?
» What did the father say to his son?
» When the son understands what the father said?
» How is the nature of the characters in that story?

35. kegunaan kata much dalam bahasa inggris


Much
Much memilki arti yang sama dengan many yaitu banyak. Lalu apa perbedaannya dengan kata many? Jika many digunakan untuk benda-benda yang dapat dihitung maka much digunakan untuk benda-benda yang tidak dapat dihitung. Contoh penggunaannya dalam suatu kalimat adalah:
I have so much coffee in my cabinet.
Karena kata coffee termasuk dalam kata benda yang tidak dapat dihitung, maka kita menggunakan much untuk menjelaskan jumlahnya.
