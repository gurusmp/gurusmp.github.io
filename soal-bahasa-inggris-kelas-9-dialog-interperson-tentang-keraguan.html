21. Percakapan bahasa inggris singkat tentang keragu-raguan


Pembahasan

Percakapan Bahasa Inggris singkat tentang keragu-raguan adalah

Didi and Lala are classmates. They are having a conversation in front of their class during a break.

Didi: Hi, Lala. Why do you look so confused these days?

Lala: Hi, Di. My parents will move to Medan.They ask me to join them and continue my study there.

Didi : Oh, that's great. Will all your family move to Medan next semester?

Lala : No, they will not move next semester. My parents and my little brother will move next week. I am not sure I can join them because I must finish the final examination.

Didi: Maybe you can stay with your aunt during the examination then after that you can move to Medan.

Lala: I am not certain about it. Sometimes, my aunt has to go out of town for her business. But I will discuss this with my parents. Thanks.

Didi: You are welcome. Anyway, that's great to hear that you will study in Medan. But, we will miss you here.  

Lala: I will miss you all too but maybe we still have couple months until I move. So, that's OK. Well, let's go to the canteen before the break is over, shall we?

Didi: OK, let's go.

26. Percakapan bahasa inggris singkat tentang keragu-raguan


Expressing Doubt / Uncertainty

Ungkapan yang menyatakan keraguan (expression of doubt/uncertainty) bisa berupa beberapa kalimat berikut:

I don't know. ...I'm not sure/certain. ...It is (quite) unlikely ...It seems (quite) unlikely ...I don't think ...I doubt ...It's doubtful that ...... may ...... will probably ...Perhaps ...
Pembahasan

Berikut contoh percakapan singkat dalam bahasa Inggris yang mengandung ungkapan keraguan:

Andi: Hi, Sandy. Do you know that George has been selected to take part in the science olympiad competition?

Sandy: Hi, Andi. Are you sure about that? Where did you hear it?

Andi: Our biology teacher, Mr. Hasan, told me this morning.

Sandy: What did he say exactly?

Andi: He said the teachers had decided to choose George to represent our school in this competition.

Sandy: I don't think you have heard it correctly. George is not even in the top 20 in our school.

Andi: That's what I heard.

Sandy: It seems unlikely that George has been selected. I think Mr. Hasan might have said 'Josh' and you misheard it.

Andi: I'm not so sure now, but I think that makes more sense. Josh is one of the top students, and he is really good at science.

Sandy: Well, if they had really selected George, he should have been informed by now. Why don't we find George and ask him?

Andi: You're right. Let's go his classroom at the 2nd recess then.

37. Percakapan mengungkapkan dan menanggapi keraguan dalam bahasa inggris. Jelaskan?


Percakapan Menungkapkan dan Menanggapi Keraguan (Dialogue About Expressing Doubt and Responding to Doubt)

Kirishima: Hey, Saeki.
Saeki: Hello, Kiri! I haven't seen you all day. How are you?
Kirishima: I'm fine, thanks. I've been busy, that's why. What about you?
Saeki: I see. I'm fine, too.
Kirishima: So, where are you going?
Saeki: I'm trying to find Maki. Have you seen her?
Kirishima: No. Maybe she's in the library?
Saeki: I'm not sure about that. She doesn't like reading.
Kirishima: Nevermind, then. What about the cafeteria?
Saeki: Ok, perhaps she's there. I'll go check.
Kirishima: Alright. See you later.
Saeki: Bye bye!

~

Buat jaga-jaga:
CONTOH MENGUNGKAPKAN KERAGUAN (EXPRESSING DOUBT):
-Well...let me see.
-I doubt it/I'm doubtful.
-I doubt that/I'm not quite sure about that.

CONTOH MENANGGAPI KERAGUAN (RESPONDING TO DOUBT):
-That's fine, then.
-You doubt it, don't you?
-You'd better confirm it.
