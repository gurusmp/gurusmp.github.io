14. if you [come] earlier they [tell] them about the news rubah kedalam conditonal type2


if you came earlier they would tell them about the newsMAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
MATERI : CONDITIONAL SENTENCE TYPE 2
KELAS : 10
KODE SOAL : 10.5
KODE KATEGORISASI : 10.5.3

PEMBAHASAN
=================
Bentuk conditional type 2 dari kalimat "if you [come] earlier they [tell] them about the news" adalah..

Berikut adalah rumus dari conditional type 2 :
If + simple past, conditional (=would + V1)

So, the answer is :
If you (CAME) earlier they (WOULD TELL) them about the news.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat Third Conditional dapat digunakan untuk menyesali sesuatu yang sudah terjadi dan sudah tidak bisa dirubah.

Third Conditional memiliki rumus:

If + S + had + Verb 3, S + would + have + Verb 3

Salah satu contoh kalimat yang mengandung kata "submit":

If I had submitted my resume on time, I would have gotten the job.

24. First conditionalMaken the sentences of first conditonal based on the function below1. warning something2. predicting3. advising4. planning5. promisingWrite your sentences on your emyschool​


Jawaban

If you forget to submit your assignment today, Mr. Schmidt will give you a severe punishment.  If more people know about this bay, it will soon become one of recommended tourism destinations. If you exercise regularly, you will lose weight.  If my relatives are not here tomorrow, I will go with my friends.  If I pass that store, I will buy the chocolate for you.

Pembahasan

If conditional tipe pertama dapat digunakan untuk manyatakan:

Prediksi masa depan – If he doesn’t come, the meeting will be postponed again. Memberi nasihat – If you have a problem with the lesson, you should go to the tutorial class. Membuat rencana – If my father lets me use the car, I will go to the party. Membuat janji – If I have time, I will call and pick you up at school. Memberi peringatan – If you touch the dog now, it will bite you.        

   

Struktur:  

          If + Present simple/continuous/perfect, + Subject + will + Verb 1

 

Penerapan:  

Untuk membuat kalimat kondisional tipe pertama, perhatikan pola struktur kalimat dan pikirkan ide yang sesuai dengan fungsi yang diminta (memberi peringatan, memprediksi, menasihati, merencanakan, membuat janji). Hasilnya seperti jawaban di atas.
