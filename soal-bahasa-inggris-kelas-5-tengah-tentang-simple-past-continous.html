1. bagaimana rumus bahasa inggris simple past continous


Rumus Past Continuous Tense

Untuk membentuk kalimatnya, kita harus menerapkan rumus bentuk lampau dari be + present participle (-ing) pada kata kerja utama.
BentukRumus Past Continuous TenseContoh KalimatPositif
(+)Subjek + be (was/were) + verb (-ing) + …She was waiting for you yesterday
They were discussing my birthday party
Negatif
(-)Subjek +be (was/were) + not + verb (-ing) + …She was not waiting for you yesterday
They were not discussing my birthday party
Interrogatif
(?)Be (was/were) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?Was she waiting for you yesterday?
Were they discussing my birthday party?

(+) subjek+be(was/were)+verb(-ing)+...
(-) subjek+be(was/were)+not+verb(-ing)+...

3. Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous adalah:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.  

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.

Kalau Pasangan "to be" nya:

I = Am

She = Is

He = Is

It = Is

We = Are

They = Are

Lebih dari satu orang = Are

I and my friends = Are

Kalau Verb atau Predikat atau Kata Kerjanya untuk present continuous:

Note: Continuous maksudnya adalah kegiatannya sedang dilakukan.

Ini tinggal tambahin "ing" di belakang kata kerjanya:

ngomong = lagi + ngomong = lagi ngomong-ngomong

speak      =   speak + ing      = speaking

nyanyi = lagi + nyanyi = lagi menyanyi

sing    = sing + ing   = singing

nari       = lagi + nari = lagi menari

dance = dance + ing = dancing

note: ada aturan; bila kata kerja diakhiri huruf "e" maka huruf "e"nya dimusnahkan saja, dan tinggal tambahin "ing" di belakannya (eg: "fire" menjadi "firing", "bake" menjadi "baking")

pengecualian beberapa kata irregular kayak:

mati = lagi + mati = lagi dalam proses mati

die = die + ing = dying - ini dia malah ganti "ie" pada "die" dengan "y"

tinta = lagi + tinta = lagi meninta (mewarnai dengan tinta)

dye = dye + ing = dyeing - ini huruf "e" pada "dye" malah gk dihilangin dan langsung ditambahin "ing", hanya supaya katanya gk jadi sama dengan kata irregular "dying" yang di atas tadi.

Kemudian ada lagi aturan, khusus kata kerja yang huruf belakannya "c"

panik = lagi + panik = lagi panik

panic = panic + ing = panicking

Jadi, perlu ditambahin huruf "k" sebelum "ing" itu.

Dan kemudian ada lagi aturan; Verbs ending with a single vowel plus a consonant.. Ini agak susah aku jelasin sekarang, karena dalam aturan ini, ada banyak variabel yang perlu diperhatikan.

Jawaban Soal:

Maka, untuk jawab soal itu, kita hanya perlu tambahin "to be" dan "verb+ing".

Seperti:

I _____ (fly) in the sky

dijawab:

I am flying in the sky - "am" karena itu to be nya "I", dan "flying" karena "fly+ing"

She _____ (jump) using a trampoline

She is jumping using a trampoline - "is" karena itu to be nya "She", dan "jumping" karena "jump + ing"

Aku lihat semua soal itu mengikuti aturan simple ini aja kok; to be + verb(ing). Jadi "note" aku yang di atas tidak dibutuhkan untuk menjawab soal ini.

Kalimat Active :

1. Simple Present : He reads the book in library.

2. Simple past : He read the book in library.

3. Simple future : He will read the book in library.

5. Past Continuous : He was reading the book in library.

6. Present Perfect : He has read the book in library.

7. Past Perfect : He had read the book in library.

Kalimat Passive :

1. Simple Present : The book is read by him in library.

2. Simple past : The book was read by him in library.

3. Simple future : The book will be read by him in library.

4. Present continuous : The book is being read by him in library.

5. Past continuous : The book was being read by him in library

6. present perfect : The book has been read by him in library.

7. past perfect : The book had been read by him in Library

18. tolong buatkan cerita bahasa inggris yang mengandung simple future tense,simple past future tense,dan present continous tense


Explanation:

- Simple future tenseThis tense is used to tell about an action that will be happened in the future.The structure: S + will + V1 + O
For example, I will go to Bali next month.

- Simple past future tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which would be happened in the past. This tense is mostly used to show wish or hope.
The structure: S + would + V1 + O
For example, I wish he would forgive me.

- Present continuous tenseThis tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + V-ing + O
For example, She is reading a book in the library.

Answer:

Yesterday, my father asked me what I would like to be in the future. He said that I had to be able to make a decision because it was related to determining which university that I would choose to continue my study. My father's question made me realize that I had to think about my future. When I was little, i would say I wanted to be a doctor. But the condition is different now. Being a doctor needs a large amount of money and intelligence. Moreover, being a doctor is also a risky job because people's life is in your hand. I can't take such responsibility. I'm thinking about it all day. I want a job which I can arrange the schedule the way I like. After asking many people and searching on the internet, I decide I want to learn foreign languages. I will be a multilingual translator!

THE PRESENT TENSE The present tense has four forms :

1.   The simple present tense

2.   The present continuous tense

3.   The present perfect tense

4.   The present perfect continuous tense

1.         The simple present tense

The formula :

I/we/you/they He/she/it INFINITIVE INFINITIVE + S It is used do denote : a.  A habitual action (every day action) b.  Unchanging truths about the universe or nature

Example   : - John walks to school (John is in habit of walk-ing to school).

                  - The grass is green (it is unchanging law of nature, that the colour of grass always green).

                  - A aeroplane flies in the sky (it is a simple fact of that an aeroplane can't fly on the ground, they always fly in the sky).

Note        : 1. A verb third singular takes an "s"

                  2. Some verbs in the simple present tense are often qualified by adverbs like :

a.  always, usually, sometimes, often, sel-dom, never and generally. These are placed before the principle verbs, but they are placed after to be.

Example   : - He always gets up early

                - Juliet is always in the room

b.         every day, every morning, every week, every night, etc. These are placed at the beginning of sentence or at the end of it

Example   : - We study every day

                  - Every morning we go to school

2.         The present Continuou Tense

The formula :

TO BE (am, are, is) + PRESENT PARTICIPLE (ING-FORM) It is used to denote an action which is still in progress at the moment of speaking. The rule to form It : ............. to be + verb + ing.

Example  : - The moon is shining now

                  - He is taking a book

Note       : 1.  When we use the present contiuous tense we are not concerned about when an action be-gins when it will end.

                  2.  Certain verbs are practically never used in the present continuous, even when describing the real present.

a.  They are :

                                notice           believe               refuse

                                certain          think                   like

                                own              have                   remember

                                love              appear               fear

                                want             consist of           ...... etc.

                  3.  The present continuous tense can also be used for an action in the immediate future to show intention.

The formula :

I/we/you/they He/she/it + PAST TENSE 

Example   : - At one o'clock yesterday, you were having lunch.

It is used to denote an action which began and ended in the past, or to denote that an action happened, or was fi-nished at some time in the past.

Example  : - I studied English diligently yesterday

                  - Andrew found his pen two days ago

Note       : 1.  The simple past tense in regular verb is for-med by adding "ed" to the infinitive :

Infinitive        Simple past

to work           worked

Infinitive        Simple past

               : 3.  There are certain verbs which are not added by "d" or "ed" those are irregular verbs, and you are to memorize them.

                  4.  Yesterday, last and ago are adverbs of time that quality the simple past tense.

The formula :

I/we You/they/he/she/it SHALL + INFINITIVE WILL + INFINITIVE

It is used to denote an action happening in future.

Example  : - I shall do my work tomorrow

                  - They will watch football match this after noon

Note       : a.  To form the simple future tense, usually we use "shall" for the first persons (I and we) and "will" for the other pronouns and pronouns of nouns.

Simple Present = dipakai buat mengatakan keadaan yang terjadi secara biasa, sehari-hari kamu lakuin, atau fakta yang udah umum orang tau. "I eat" kamu makan, dan kamu makan nggak cuma sekali, kamu makan tiap hari sampe kamu pulang ke surga, bener ga.
"A cat meows" kucing mengeong, dan itu fakta.

Present Continuous = sebutannya "present progressive", dipakai buat kegiatan yang kamu lakuin sekarang dan mungkin masih akan kamu lakuin sampe berapa waktu di masa depan. "You are eating" aku sedang makan. Bisa aja kamu makan sampe sepuluh menit ke depan, atau seharian juga boleh, makannya itu akan selesai suatu saat di masa depan tapi yang penting sekarang ini kamu bilang kamu lagi makan.

Present Perfect = menunjukkan kegiatan yang udah kamu lakuin dari masa lalu tapi belum selesai di masa sekarang. "I have not spoken" saya belum bicara. Berarti dulu saya nggak bicara dan sampai sekarang pun saya nggak bicara. "He has eaten" dia sudah makan. Berarti dia dulu udah makan dan sampai sekarang udah makan.

Present Perfect Continuous = ini hampir sama kayak yang atasnya ini, tapi bedanya, dia belum selesai dan masih berlanjut (continue). "I have been waiting" saya udah menunggu. Maksudnya dulu saya nunggu dan sampai sekarang saya masih nunggu. Di masa depan apa masih? Nggak tau, pokoknya sekarang masih, gitu.


Simple Past = artinya, ada satu kegiatan yang kamu lakuin di masa lalu dan udah selesai juga di masa lalu, tapi kamu merujuk ke satu waktu spesifik di mana kamu ngelakuin itu. Urutannya, mereka makan, terus saya ngomong.

Past Continuous = ini sejenis tense nomer dua kamu yang di atas itu, tapi dilakuinnya di masa lalu. "I was studying when she called" Nggak terlalu perlu dikasih keterangan juga, tapi saya mau bilang kalau pas si dia tadi manggil, saya tu lagi belajar. Belajarnya udah tadi, dia manggilnya pas saya belajar tadi. Urutannya, saya belajar, terus pas belajar dia manggil, terus saya selesai belajar dan saya ngomong.

Past Perfect = kalau ini saya kasih contoh dulu aja ya: "We had been together before we met Harry" kami udah bersama sebelum kami ketemu kamu. Future Perfect Continuous = "Next year, you will have been living for 20 years" taun depan kamu bakalan sudah hidup selama 20 taun. Explanation:

- Simple present tense
This tense is used to tell about an action that happens at the present time or habitual activity. It also can be used to tell about general truth.
The structure: S + V1 (+ s/es) + O

- Present continuous tense
This tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + O

- Present perfect tense
This tense is used to tell that the action is completely done in an unspecific time before now.
The structure: S + has/have + V3 + O

- Present perfect continuous tense
This tense is used to tell that the action starts at an unspecific time in the past and continue to the present.
The structure: S + has/have + been + O

- Simple past tense
This tense is used to indicate an action that is happened in the past. It began and ended in the past.
The structure: S + V2 + O

- Past continuous tense
This tense is used to indicate an action that was in progress at a particular time in the past.
The structure: S + to be (was/were) + V-ing + O

- Past perfect tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which was completely done before another time in the past.
The structure: S + had + V3 + O

- Past perfect continuous tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which was in progress before another event in the past. This tense is used to show the duration of the first event.
The structure: S + had + been + V-ing + O
