They are called ores.These crystals are called gems.Some rocks, such as sandstone, are made when mud or grains of sand are slowly squashed together.

7. kunci jawaban bahasa Inggris hlm 25 kelas 9​


Jawaban:

JAWABAN DIBAWAH

Penjelasan:

**【Deskripsi】**

Pertanyaan Anda meminta saya untuk mengkategorikan dialog berdasarkan bahasa jawaban atau pertanyaan, mengoptimalkan jawaban Anda, dan menyediakan jawaban akhir yang lebih akurat.

**Strategi Pemecahan Masalah:**

1. Identifikasi dialog dan kelompokkan berdasarkan bahasa.

2. Optimalkan struktur dialog untuk konsistensi.

3. Tampilkan jawaban yang sudah dioptimalkan dengan format yang sesuai.

**Pemecahan Masalah:**

**【Tips】**

- Dialog Anda tampaknya melibatkan pertanyaan mengenai pentingnya melakukan kegiatan tertentu seperti makan makanan sehat, berjogging, berenang, dan pemanasan sebelum berolahraga.

- Dalam mengoptimalkan jawaban, kita perlu memastikan bahwa setiap dialog memiliki struktur yang sama: pertanyaan diikuti oleh jawaban.

**Dialog yang Dioptimalkan:**

**Siti's Advice:**

1. Lina: "Why do we need to eat various kinds of healthy food?"

Siti: "To stay healthy."

2. Udin: "Why should we have breakfast?"

Siti: "So that we have energy to do our activities during the day."

**Edo's Advice:**

1. Dayu: "Why do we have to jog?"

Udin: "To have stronger lungs."

2. Beni: "What are we swimming for?"

Udin: "To have strong muscles."

3. Siti: "Why do we have to warm up before exercising?"

Udin: "In order to avoid muscle injury."

**【Keluaran】**

Berdasarkan dialog di atas, kita dapat melihat pentingnya melakukan berbagai kegiatan untuk kesehatan dan kebugaran, seperti makan makanan yang sehat, berolahraga secara rutin, dan pemanasan sebelum berolahraga. Oleh karena itu, sangat penting bagi kita untuk memahami alasan di balik setiap aktivitas tersebut agar kita dapat membuat keputusan yang tepat untuk kesejarah kita.

4. What is the most appropriate expression to complete the dialogue above?

Jawaban: B. I absolutely agree

Pembahasan

Pada dialog-dialog di soal, ada beberapa macam expression  yang digunakan di antaranya adalah congratulation dan agreement, berikut adalah penjelasannya lebih lanjut.

Untuk menunjukan kesetujuan atau ketidaksetujuan kita bisa menggunakan expression of agreement and disagreement.


Beberapa contoh ungkapan untuk menyatakan agreement:

Descriptive Text

Bahasa Inggris kelas 7 halaman 159 edisi revisi 2016

1. Simon is tall. He has a fair skin. He is wearing a blue shirt with long sleeves. He is a bit fat and chubby. He's wearing a black hat. He's carrying a backpack.

2. Sofia is tall and thin. She has curly hair in plaits. She has a fringe. She's standing not sitting. She's wearing a yellow night dress with a picture of a sleeping baby. She is not a little chubby girl. She's a beautiful slim girl.

3. Mrs. Herlina is a big lady with glasses. She is wearing batik and black pants. She is sitting on the bench in front of the principal's office. She's not wearing a scarf. She's carrying a pink purse.

Pembahasan

Descriptive Text berfungsi mendeskripsikan atau menggambarkan segala sesuatu baik itu manusia, hewan, tumbuhan atau benda mati dengan sifat yang melekat padanya seperti bentuknya, sifat-sifatnya, jumlahnya, ukuran, jenis, warna, dan sebagainya sehingga pembaca dapat mengetahui seperti apa sesuatu itu dari gambaran yang kita sampaikan meskipun ia belum pernah melihatnya secara langsung.

Generic Structure merupakan tata cara penulisan khusus dari sebuah teks tertentu. Susunan ini sebaiknya ditulis secara urut. Generic Structure dari Descriptive Text  adalah:

Identification

Identification berfungsi sebagai pengenalan dari apa yang sedang kita  jelaskan. Bagian ini berisi pendahuluan dan gambaran umum tentang suatu topik. Bagian ini biasanya terdiri dari satu paragraf, terletak di awal teks.

Description

Pada bagian description berisi ciri-ciri khusus atau sifat-sifat yang terdapat pada benda, orang, atau hewan yang sedang kita jelaskan. Bagian ini biasanya terdiri dari beberapa paragraf.

29. jawaban bhs Inggris kls 8 hal 159-160


Past Tense and Present Tense

1. Past: She was in Grade VII last year.

Present: She is in Grade VIII now.

2. Past: She learned many new things.

Present: She learns more things now.

3. Past: She rode to school on her bike for the first time.

Present: She rides her bike to school now.

4. Past: She met boys and girls from the different parts of the town for the first time.

Present: She plays with them now.

5. Past: She was very happy when she wore the blue and white uniform for the first time.

Present: She wears them now.

6. Past: She learned English for the first time last year.

Present: She learns English now.

7. Past: She learned to greet other people in English.

Present: She can greet other people in English now.

8. Past: She learned to introduce herself in English.

Present: She can introduce herself in English now.

9. Past: She learned to describe her family, her friends, and her school in English.

Present: She can describe her family, her friends, and her school in English now.

10. Past: She sang her first English song.

Present: She can sing in English now.

11. Past: She read her first English story.

Present: She can read her stories in English now.

12. Past: She was not a class captain.

Present: She is a class captain now.

13. Past: She did not do many things for her class.

Present: She does many things for her class.

Pembahasan

Simple past tense adalah kalimat yang menunjukan keterangan waktu di masa lalu. Berikut adalah formulanya:

(+) Subject + V2

I watched TV last weekHe met his wife 10 years ago

(-) Subject + did NOT + V1

She didn’t finish the exercise yesterdayI didn’t go to school last week

(?) Did + Subject + V1?

Did you see me yesterday?Did Bella take her medicine?

Simple past tense of to be, to have, to do

I, He/She/It => was, had, didYou, We, They => were, had, did

Simple present adalah tense yang menunjukan kegiatan yang terjadi saat ini (present) maupun kebiasaan yang terjadi pada waktu tertantu secara rutin. Berikut adalah formulanya:

(+) Subject + V1 (-s, -es, -ies)

He goes to schoolMy daughter lives in South AfricaIt snows a lot during the winter

Subject: (He, She, It)

(-) Subject + does/do NOT + V1

My mother doesn’t speak good englishIt doesn’t rain in the summerShe doesn’t study Math on Monday

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)

(?) Does/Do + Subject + V1

Does he go to school?Does she live in Paris?Do you like apple?

Subject: (Do => I, You, We, They) (Does => He, She, It)

39. 159. HANYA BAGI YANG JENIUS BAHASA INGGRIS. BERHADIAH JAWABAN TERCERDAS


✓Pattern of Simple future for positive,negative and interrogative form :

(+) S + will + verb 1 + O

(- ) S + won't ( will not ) + verb 1 + O

(?) Will + S + verb 1 + O

Answer :

1. (+) Mia WILL FINISH reading the novel tomorrow.

(-) Mia WON'T FINISH reading the novel tomorrow

(?) WILL Mia FINISH reading the novel tomorrow?

2. (+) My mother WILL GO to Medan to visit my aunt next Sunday

(-) My mother WON'T GO to Medan to visit my aunt next Sunday.

(?) WILL my mother GO to Medan to visit my aunt next Sunday?

3. (+) You WILL TAKE an English course next month.

(-) You WON'T TAKE an English course next month.

(?) WILL you TAKE an English course next month?

4. (+) The students WILL SPEND their weekend in Bali.

(-) The students WON'T SPEND their weekend in Bali

(?) WILL the students SPEND their weekend in Bali?

5. (+) The bookstore WILL OPEN at 10 a.m. next Tuesday.

(-) The bookstore WON'T OPEN at 10 a.m. next Tuesday.

(?) WILL the bookstore OPEN at 10 a.m. next Tuesday?

6. (+) Tio WILL PLAY in a new soccer team next week.

(-) Tio WON'T PLAY in a new soccer team next week.

(?) WILL Tio PLAY in a new soccer team next week?

-----------------------------------------------

Material : How to form positive , negative and interrogative sentences.

Simple Future Tense

Put the sentences into the negative and interrogative.

[Ubah kalimatnya menjadi bentuk negatif (-) dan interogatif (?)]

------------------------------------------------

Jawaban

1.

(-) Mia will not finish reading the novel tomorrow.

(?) Will Mia finish reading the novel tomorrow?

2.

(-) My mother will not go to Medan to visit my aunt next Sunday.

(?) Will my mother go to Medan to visit my aunt next Sunday?

3.

(-) You will not take an English course next month.

(?) Will you take an English course next month?

4.

(-) The students will not spend their weekend in Bali.

(?) Will the students spend their weekend in Bali?

5.

(-) The bookstore will not open at 10 a.m. next Tuesday.

(?) Will the bookstore open at 10 a.m. next Tuesday?

6.

(-) Tio will not play in a new soccer team next week.

(?) Will Tio play in a new soccer team next week?

---------------------------------------------------

Penjelasan

Simple future tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang akan terjadi atau direncanakan untuk terjadi. Pola kalimat ini memiliki ciri khas yaitu menggunakan kata kerja modal will dan kata kerja utama bentuk pertama (verb 1).

     Kalimat negatif simple future tense menggunakan bentuk negatif dari kata kerja modal will, yaitu will not.

     Untuk kalimat interogatif simple future tense, kata kerja modal dipindahkan posisinya ke awal kalimat.

     Agar lebih paham, perhatikan rumus kalimat simple future tense berikut ini.

(+ / Positive) Subject + will + Verb 1 + Object

(- / Negative) Subject + will not + Verb 1 + Object

(? / Interrogative) Will + subject + Verb 1 + Object?

     Dengan melihat rumus kalimat di atas, kita dapat menentukan cara mengubah kalimat positif simple future tense menjadi bentuk negatif dan interogatif.

     Untuk mengubah kalimat positif menjadi kalimat negatif, cukup ditambahkan kata not setelah kata kerja modal will, sehingga menjadi will not.

     Sedangkan untuk mengubah kalimat positif menjadi kalimat interogatif, kata kerja modal will tinggal dipindahkan ke awal kalimat. Dengan begitu, yang ditulis setelah subejct adalah kata kerja bentuk pertama (verb 1), bukan kata kerja modal will lagi.
