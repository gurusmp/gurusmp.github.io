Décrire une personne

Je voudrais parler d'un ami de moi. Il s'appelle Louis. Il est un jeune garçon de seize ans. Il est grand. Il mesure un mètre soixante-cinq. Il n'est ni gros ni maigre.

Il est un beau garçon. Il a le visage rectangulaire, les grands yeux ronds, et le nez pointu. Il a la peau hâlée. Son teint est frais, et ses dents sont blanches. Il a les cheveux bruns et les yeux bleus.

Il est athlétique. Il aime les sports. Il joue au football et au basket-ball. Il est calme, attentif, et indépendant. Il est aussi chaleureux, donc il a beaucoup d'amis.

Saya ingin berbicara tentang seorang teman saya. Namanya Louis. Dia adalah seorang anak laki-laki berusia enam belas tahun. Dia tinggi. Tingginya satu meter enam puluh lima. Dia tidak gemuk atau kurus.

Dia adalah anak laki-laki yang tampan. Dia memiliki wajah persegi panjang, mata bulat besar, dan hidung mancung. Dia memiliki kulit berwarna kecokelatan. Mukanya segar, dan giginya putih. Dia mempunyai rambut coklat dan mata biru.

Dia atletis. Dia suka olahraga. Dia bermain sepak bola dan basket. Dia tenang, penuh perhatian dan mandiri. Dia juga ramah, jadi dia punya banyak teman.

Explication

Ketika kita mendeskripsikan seseorang, biasanya kita menggambarkan fisik dan sifat/karakter orang tersebut.

Ada dua struktur kalimat sederhana yang dapat kita gunakan, yaitu:

personne + être + adjectif

Ex. Je suis petite - saya pendek.

personne + avoir + nom

Ex. J'ai les cheveux longs - saya mempunyai rambut panjang.

COVID-19

COVID-19 is a new illness that can affect your lungs and airways. It’s caused by a type of coronavirus. COVID-19, like the flu, can be spread from person to person. When a person who has COVID-19 coughs, sneezes or talks, they may spread droplets containing the virus a short distance, which quickly settle on surrounding surfaces.

What can you do to stop COVID-19 spreading? First, cough or sneeze into your elbow or by covering your mouth and nose with tissues. Put used tissues in the bin or a bag immediately. Wash your hands with soap and water often (for at least 20 seconds). Try to avoid close contact with people who are unwell. Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth if your hands are not clean. Avoid personal contact, such as kissing, sharing cups or food with sick people. Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces and objects, such as doorknobs. And the last, stay home if you feel unwell.

Candhi Barabudhur

Candhi Barabudhur mujudake candhi Buddha kang digawe ing abad IX dening Raja Semaratungga sing ngasta kraton Mataram Hindhu. Raja Semaratungga saka wangsa Syailendra. Candi Borobudur

Candi Borobudur merupakan candi Buddha yang dibangun pada abad IX oleh Raja Samaratungga yang menjadi raja Kerajaan Mataram Hindu. Raja Samaratungga berasal dari dinasti Syailendra. Candi ini merupakan candi Buddha terbesar di dunia. Tepatnya, Candi Borobudur terletak di sebelah barat daya Kota Muntilan, Kabupaten Magelang, Jawa Tengah.

Candi Borobudur terdiri dari sembilan bagian yaitu enam teras berbentuk persegi dan tiga berbentuk lingkaran dengan puncaknya berwujud stupa di tengah. Candi ini dihiasi panel relief berjumlah 2.672 dan patung Buddha sebanyak 504 buah. Stupa tengah dikelilingi patung Buddha yang sedang duduk berjumlah 72 yang diletakkan di dalam stupa-stupa berlubang.

Sampai sekarang, Candi Borobudur masih menjadu tempat ibadah. Penganut Buddha merayakan Waisak di candi ini setiap tahun. Candi Borobudur juga sudah terkenal menjadi tempat wisata yang membuat takjub wisatawan Indonesia dan mancanegara.

Paragraf 1 (Identifikasi):

The pineapple, also known as Ananas comosus, is a tropical fruit that belongs to the Bromeliaceae family. It is characterized by its unique appearance, with a rough, spiky skin and a crown of spiky leaves on top. The fruit itself is cylindrical in shape, with a yellowish-brown outer skin and a juicy, sweet, and tangy flesh inside. Pineapples are native to South America, particularly Brazil and Paraguay, but they are now grown in many tropical regions around the world.

Paragraf 2 (Deskriptif):

A ripe pineapple is a sight to behold. Its rough, textured skin is a mix of golden yellow and brown hues, giving it a vibrant and inviting appearance. As you run your fingers along the surface, you can feel the sharp, prickly spikes that protect the succulent flesh within. The crown of leaves on top adds a touch of natural elegance to this tropical gem.

Once you slice through the tough skin, a burst of sweet aroma fills the air. The juicy flesh reveals itself, displaying a sunny yellow color with occasional fibrous strands. Each bite is a delightful combination of sweetness and tanginess, making your taste buds dance with joy. The juice is refreshing and invigorating, leaving a lingering tropical flavor on your palate.

The pineapple's flavor is unique and versatile, making it a perfect addition to both sweet and savory dishes. It can be enjoyed fresh as a snack, added to fruit salads, or blended into refreshing smoothies. Grilled pineapple slices are a popular treat, offering a caramelized sweetness that pairs well with savory dishes like barbecued meats.

In addition to its delicious taste, the pineapple is also packed with nutrients. It contains high levels of vitamin C, manganese, and bromelain, an enzyme known for its anti-inflammatory properties. These nutrients contribute to its reputation as a healthful fruit that supports digestion, boosts the immune system, and promotes overall well-being.

In conclusion, the pineapple is a tropical fruit that captivates with its unique appearance, enticing aroma, and delicious flavor. Whether enjoyed on its own or incorporated into various dishes, it is a true delight for the senses. Its nutritional benefits further enhance its appeal, making it a favored choice for those seeking a healthy and flavorful treat.

Pineapple, also known as Ananas comosus, is a tropical fruit that is easily recognizable by its unique appearance and sweet tropical aroma. When you hold a ripe pineapple in your hands, you can feel the prickly texture of its skin.
